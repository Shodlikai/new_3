Ko'chirma So'roq Gaplar Qoidalari

Embedded Question nima?

Embedded Question - bu boshqa bir darak yoki so'roq gapning ichiga joylashtirilgan so'roq gapdir. Ular gapni yanada xushmuomalali yoki murakkabroq qilish uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Oddiy so'roq: Where is the station? (Stansiya qayerda?)
  • Ko'chirma so'roq: Could you tell me where the station is? (Stansiya qayerdaligini ayta olasizmi?)

1-qoida: Darak gap tartibi (Statement Word Order)

Bu eng muhim qoida: ko'chirma so'roq gapning ichki qismi har doim darak gap tartibida (Ega + Kesim) bo'ladi, so'roq gap tartibida emas. Yordamchi fe'llar do/does/did ishlatilmaydi.

To'g'ri: I don't know where she lives. (Ega 'she' + Kesim 'lives')

Xato: I don't know where does she live.

2-qoida: if va whether ishlatilishi

Agar ko'chirilayotgan so'roq gap "Ha/Yo'q" (Yes/No) turiga mansub bo'lsa (ya'ni, maxsus so'roq so'zi bilan boshlanmasa), ko'chirma so'roq gapda uni bog'lash uchun if yoki whether so'zlari ishlatiladi.

Oddiy so'roq: Is she coming? (U kelyaptimi?)

Ko'chirma so'roq: I wonder if she is coming. (U kelayotganmikan, qiziqyabman).

To'g'ridan-to'g'ri va Ko'chirma so'roq gaplar farqi

1. So'z tartibi farqi (WH- savollarida)

Asosiy farq so'z tartibidadir. To'g'ridan-to'g'ri so'roqlarda yordamchi fe'l egadan oldin keladi, ko'chirma so'roqlarda esa egadan keyin keladi (darak gap kabi).

  • To'g'ridan-to'g'ri: So'roq so'zi + Yordamchi fe'l + Ega + Kesim? (Why did he leave?)
  • Ko'chirma: Boshlovchi ibora + So'roq so'zi + Ega + Kesim. (I don't know why he left.)

Direct: What time is it?

Embedded: Do you know what time it is?

Farq: 'is it' (so'roq tartibi) 'it is' (darak tartibi) ga o'zgardi.

2. "Ha/Yo'q" savollaridagi farq

To'g'ridan-to'g'ri "Ha/Yo'q" savollari yordamchi fe'l bilan boshlanadi. Ko'chirma so'roqlarda esa bu yordamchi fe'l o'rniga if yoki whether ishlatiladi va darak gap tartibiga o'tiladi.

  • To'g'ridan-to'g'ri: Yordamchi fe'l + Ega + Kesim? (Does she speak Greek?)
  • Ko'chirma: Boshlovchi ibora + if/whether + Ega + Kesim. (I'd like to know if she speaks Greek.)

Direct: Did you call your mom?

Embedded: He asked if you called your mom.

Farq: 'Did' yordamchi fe'li tushib qoldi va uning o'rniga 'if' qo'shildi. Fe'l 'call' o'tgan zamonga ('called') o'zgardi.

3. Punktuatsiya (tinish belgilari) farqi

Gapning oxiridagi tinish belgisi to'g'ridan-to'g'ri so'roqqa emas, balki butun gapni boshlab kelgan asosiy qismga bog'liq bo'ladi.

  • Agar asosiy gap darak bo'lsa, oxiriga nuqta (.) qo'yiladi.
  • Agar asosiy gap so'roq bo'lsa, oxiriga so'roq belgisi (?) qo'yiladi.

I don't know where she is. (Bosh gap "I don't know" - darak gap).

Could you tell me where she is? (Bosh gap "Could you tell me" - so'roq gap).

Ko'chirma So'roq Gap Tuzilmalari

WH- so'zlar bilan

Boshlovchi ibora + WH-so'z + Ega + Kesim...

I don't know where she lives.

Men uning qayerda yashashini bilmayman.

Could you tell me how much it costs?

Bu qancha turishini ayta olasizmi?

If / Whether bilan

Boshlovchi ibora + if/whether + Ega + Kesim...

He is asking if this seat is free.

U bu o'rindiq bo'sh yoki yo'qligini so'rayapti.

I wonder whether they will get married.

Ular turmush qurishadimi yoki yo'qmi, qiziqyabman.

Misollar bilan tanishing

WH-savollar bilan

I don't know how they got the idea.

Ular bu g'oyani qanday olganini bilmayman.

Could you tell me why you did it?

Nima uchun bunday qilganingizni ayta olasizmi?

He asked for the name of the person who had won the award.

U mukofotni yutgan insonning ismini so'radi.

"Ha/Yo'q" savollari bilan (if/whether)

I don't know if they have found a flat.

Men ular kvartira topgan-topmaganini bilmayman.

I wonder if/whether they will be all right.

Men ularning hammalari joyida bo'lish-bo'lmasligiga qiziqaman.

She wondered whether I had read the book that was recommended to us.

U bizga maslahat berilgan kitobni o'qigan-o'qimaganimni bilishga qiziqdi.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Embedded Questions)

🚫 XATO 1: So'roq gap tartibini ishlatish
I don't know where is the station.
I don't know where the station is.

Eng asosiy qoida: Ko'chirma so'roq gap har doim darak gap tartibida (Ega + Kesim) bo'ladi.

🚫 XATO 2: `do/does/did` yordamchi fe'llarini ishlatish
Could you tell me what time does the train leave?
Could you tell me what time the train leaves?

Qoida: `do/does/did` yordamchi fe'llari ko'chirma so'roq gaplarda ishlatilmaydi. Ular tushib qoladi va asosiy fe'l zamonga moslanadi (masalan, `-s` qo'shiladi yoki o'tgan zamonga o'tadi).

🚫 XATO 3: "Ha/Yo'q" savollarida `if/whether`ni unutish
I want to know are you coming.
I want to know if you are coming.

Qoida: Agar asl savol "Ha" yoki "Yo'q" deb javob berishni talab qilsa, ko'chirma so'roq gapda `if` yoki `whether` ishlatilishi shart.

🚫 XATO 4: Noto'g'ri tinish belgisi
I wonder where he is?
I wonder where he is.

Qoida: Gapning oxiridagi tinish belgisi asosiy (bosh) gapga bog'liq. Agar bosh gap darak bo'lsa (I wonder...), oxiriga nuqta qo'yiladi.

🚫 XATO 5: Zamonlar moslashuvini unutish
He asked me where I live. (Agar "Where do you live?" deb so'ragan bo'lsa)
He asked me where I lived.

Qoida: Agar asosiy gapning fe'li (masalan, 'asked') o'tgan zamonda bo'lsa, ko'chirma so'roq gapning zamoni ham o'tgan zamonga moslashishi kerak (Present → Past).

🚫 XATO 6: `that` va `if` ni aralashtirish
I don't know that if he will come.
I don't know if he will come.

Qoida: Ko'chirma so'roq gaplarni bog'lash uchun 'that' ishlatilmaydi. 'that' faqat ko'chirma darak gaplar uchun ishlatilishi mumkin.

🚫 XATO 7: `to be` fe'lini noto'g'ri joylashtirish
Can you tell me how old is she?
Can you tell me how old she is?

Qoida: 'to be' fe'li (is, are, was, were) har doim egadan keyin keladi.

🚫 XATO 8: So'roq so'zini unutish
He asked me I wanted a drink.
He asked me if I wanted a drink.

Qoida: Barcha ko'chirma so'roq gaplar WH-so'zi yoki 'if'/'whether' bilan boshlanishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 9: `whether` dan keyin `or not` ni noto'g'ri ishlatish
I don't know whether or not he is coming. (Bu to'g'ri, lekin ...)
I don't know whether he is coming or not.

Qoida: `or not` iborasi `whether` dan keyin darhol yoki gapning oxirida kelishi mumkin. `if` bilan esa faqat gap oxirida keladi: "I don't know if he is coming or not."

🚫 XATO 10: `why` bilan `to + infinitive` ishlatish
I don't know why to do.
I don't know what to do.

Qoida: `what`, `where`, `how`, `when` kabi so'roq so'zlaridan keyin `to+infinitive` ishlatish mumkin. Lekin `why` dan keyin bu struktura ishlatilmaydi. Uning o'rniga "why I should do it" kabi to'liq gap ishlatiladi.
