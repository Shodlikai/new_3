Relative Clause Asosiy Qoidalari

Relative Clause nima va nima uchun kerak?

Relative Clause (Nisbiy Ergash Gap) - bu gapdagi biror otga (noun) qo'shimcha ma'lumot beruvchi kichik bir gapcha. U "qaysi?" degan savolga javob beradi.

  • Maqsad: Ikki sodda gapni birlashtirib, bitta murakkab va ravon gap hosil qilish. Nutqni takrorlanishlardan xalos qilish.
  • Oddiy gaplar: The man was friendly. He lives next door. (Kishi xushmuomala edi. U qo'shni uyda yashaydi.)
  • Relative Clause bilan: The man who lives next door is friendly. (Qo'shni uyda yashaydigan kishi xushmuomala.)

Who va That (Insonlar uchun)

Odamlarga ta'rif berishda who yoki that ishlatiladi. who biroz rasmiyroq hisoblanadi.

  • I know a man who can speak five languages.
  • The girl that works at the cafe is my cousin.
  • Muhim: Agar ot atoqli ot bo'lsa (masalan, John, my mother), odatda that ishlatilmaydi, faqat who ishlatiladi. Bunga "Farqlar" bo'limida batafsil to'xtalamiz.
  • Masalan: My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor. (✅ To'g'ri)
  • My brother, that lives in London, is a doctor. (❌ Odatda ishlatilmaydi)

Which va That (Narsa va Hayvonlar uchun)

Narsalar yoki hayvonlarga ta'rif berishda which yoki that ishlatiladi.

  • This is the book which I was telling you about.
  • Where is the cheese that was in the fridge?
  • The horse which won the race is very expensive.

Whom (Insonlar uchun - Obyekt)

whom - who ning rasmiy va obyekt (to'ldiruvchi) shakli. Agar ta'riflanayotgan shaxs relative clause ichida harakatni bajarmasa, balki harakat unga qaratilgan bo'lsa, whom ishlatiladi.

  • Obyekt (To'ldiruvchi) nima degani? Bu shaxs yoki narsa gapdagi harakatni bajarmaydi, balki harakatning ta'sirini qabul qiladi. Masalan, "I met the man" gapida "the man" - to'ldiruvchi, chunki u "uchrashish" harakatini bajarmayapti, "men" uni uchratdim.
  • The woman whom I met yesterday was very kind. (Men kecha uchratgan ayol juda mehribon edi.) - "uchratish" harakatini "men" bajardim, "ayol" esa to'ldiruvchi.
  • This is the professor whom I admire. (Bu men hurmat qiladigan professor.) - "hurmat qilish" harakatini "men" bajaryapman.

Whose (Qarashlilik uchun)

Biror narsaning kimga yoki nimaga tegishli ekanligini bildirish uchun whose ishlatiladi. U odamlarga ham, narsalarga ham tegishli bo'lishi mumkin.

  • Otga tegishli degani nima? Bu biror narsa (mashina, uy, fikr) boshqa bir otga (insonga, hayvonga) tegishli ekanligini, ya'ni uning bir qismi, mulki yoki qarindoshi ekanligini anglatadi.
  • The boy whose bike was stolen went to the police. (Velosipedi o'g'irlangan bola politsiyaga bordi.) - Velosiped bolaga tegishli.
  • I have a friend whose mother is a famous actress. (Mening onasi mashhur aktrisa bo'lgan do'stim bor.) - Ona do'stimga tegishli (uning onasi).
  • It's a company whose products are sold worldwide. (Bu mahsulotlari butun dunyoda sotiladigan kompaniya.) - Mahsulotlar kompaniyaga tegishli.

Strukturalar: Olmoshning Gapdagi Vazifasi

1. Olmosh - Subyekt (EGA)

Subyekt (Ega) nima? Bu relative clause ichidagi harakatni bajaruvchi shaxs yoki narsadir. Agar olmoshdan keyin to'g'ridan-to'g'ri fe'l (is, works, has, lives kabi) kelsa, demak u subyekt vazifasini bajarmoqda.

...Ot + who/which/that + Fe'l ...

Oltin Qoida: Agar nisbiy olmosh (who, which, that) relative clause'ning subyekti (egasi) bo'lsa, uni HECH QACHON TUSHIRIB QOLDIRIB BO'LMAYDI!

Misol 1:

I met a woman who works as a chef.

Tahlil: Relative clause: "who works as a chef". Bu gapchada kim ishlaydi? - "who" (ya'ni, "woman"). Demak, "who" subyekt. Uni olib tashlab bo'lmaydi.

I met a woman works as a chef. (XATO)

Misol 2:

The car that is parked outside is mine.

Tahlil: Relative clause: "that is parked outside". Nima park qilingan? - "that" (ya'ni, "car"). "that" bu yerda subyekt.

The car is parked outside is mine. (XATO)

2. Olmosh - Obyekt (TO'LDIRUVCHI)

Obyekt (To'ldiruvchi) nima? Bu relative clause ichida harakatni bajarmaydigan, balki harakat unga qaratilgan shaxs yoki narsadir. Odatda, olmoshdan keyin boshqa bir ega (I, you, he, they, the man kabi) keladi.

...Ot + (whom/which/that) + Ega + Fe'l ...

Oltin Qoida: Agar nisbiy olmosh (who, whom, which, that) relative clause'ning obyekti (to'ldiruvchisi) bo'lsa, uni TUSHIRIB QOLDIRISH MUMKIN! Bu nutqni tabiiy va ravon qiladi.

Misol 1:

The film (which) we watched was great.

Tahlil: Relative clause: "(which) we watched". Kim ko'rdi? - "we" (subyekt). Nimani ko'rdi? - "which" (ya'ni, "film") (obyekt). Demak, "which" obyekt, uni tushirib qoldirsa bo'ladi.

The film we watched was great. (✅ Juda keng tarqalgan va to'g'ri variant)

Misol 2:

The person (whom) you should talk to is Mr. Harris.

Tahlil: Relative clause: "(whom) you should talk to". Kim gaplashishi kerak? - "you" (subyekt). Kim bilan? - "whom" (ya'ni, "person") (obyekt). "whom" - obyekt, uni tushirish mumkin.

The person you should talk to is Mr. Harris. (✅ To'g'ri)

3. Olmosh - Qarashlilik (`Whose`)

Otga tegishli degani nima? Bu biror narsa (mashina, uy, fikr) boshqa bir otga (insonga, hayvonga) tegishli ekanligini, ya'ni uning bir qismi, mulki yoki qarindoshi ekanligini anglatadi.

...Ot + whose + Ot + Fe'l ...

Oltin Qoida: whose o'zidan keyin har doim ot (noun) bilan keladi va uni HECH QACHON TUSHIRIB QOLDIRIB BO'LMAYDI.

Misol 1:

She is the artist whose paintings are famous.

Tahlil: "paintings" (rasmlar) kimga tegishli? - "artist"ga. Bu bog'liqlikni "whose" ta'minlaydi. Formula: Noun + whose + Noun.

Misol 2:

I have a dog whose name is Rex.

Tahlil: "name" (ism) nimaga tegishli? - "dog"ga. "whose" bu yerda hayvonga nisbatan ham ishlatilmoqda.

10 ta Muhim Qoida va Keng Tarqalgan Xato

🚫 XATO 1: Subyekt Olmoshini Tushirib Qoldirish
The man lives next door is very kind.
The man who/that lives next door is very kind.
🔍 Sababi:

Relative clause ("...lives next door") ichida harakatni ("yashaydi") bajaruvchi ega (subyekt) yo'q. Bu vazifani who yoki that bajarishi kerak. Agar olmoshdan keyin to'g'ridan-to'g'ri fe'l (lives, works, is, has) kelsa, demak u subyekt va uni tushirib bo'lmaydi.

❌ XATO 2: Non-Defining Clause'da `that` ishlatish
My mother, that is a retired teacher, loves gardening.
My mother, who is a retired teacher, loves gardening.
🔍 Sababi:

Vergullar bilan ajratilgan, qo'shimcha ma'lumot beruvchi (Non-defining) gaplarda hech qachon that ishlatilmaydi. Odamlar uchun faqat who, narsalar uchun esa faqat which ishlatiladi.

🤔 XATO 3: Obyektni qayta takrorlash
This is the book which I bought it yesterday.
This is the book which I bought yesterday.
This is the book I bought yesterday.
🔍 Sababi:

which (yoki that) olmoshi allaqachon "it" (ya'ni, "the book") ning vazifasini bajarmoqda. Gapga yana "it", "him", "her", "them" kabi olmoshlarni qo'shish ortiqcha takrorlanish bo'ladi va bu katta grammatik xatodir.

⚙️ XATO 4: `whose` va `who's` ni adashtirish
The boy who's bike was stolen is my friend.
The boy whose bike was stolen is my friend.
🔍 Sababi:

who's bu "who is" yoki "who has" ning qisqartmasi. Masalan: Who's at the door? (Eshikda kim bor?).
whose esa egalikni bildiruvchi olmosh ("-ning"). Masalan: Whose book is this? (Bu kimning kitobi?). Qarashlilik haqida gap ketganda doim whose ishlatiladi.

📍 XATO 5: `where` o'rniga `which` ishlatish
This is the house which I grew up.
This is the house where I grew up. (yoki ...in which I grew up)
🔍 Sababi:

Agar gapda joy (location) nazarda tutilsa va harakat shu joyning "ichida" sodir bo'lsa, where ishlatiladi. where aslida "in which", "at which" kabi predlogli birikmalarning o'rnini bosadi.

🗓️ XATO 6: `when` o'rniga `which` ishlatish
I remember the day which we first met.
I remember the day when we first met. (yoki ...on which we first met)
🔍 Sababi:

Vaqtni bildiruvchi otlardan (day, year, time, moment) keyin odatda when ishlatiladi. when bu "on which" yoki "in which" ning qisqartmasi hisoblanadi.

🧐 XATO 7: Predlogdan keyin `who` yoki `that` ishlatish
The person with who I traveled was my friend.
The person with whom I traveled was my friend.
🔍 Sababi:

Predloglardan (with, for, about, to) keyin insonlarga nisbatan faqat whom ishlatilishi mumkin. who va that predlogdan keyin kela olmaydi. Bu juda rasmiy qoidadir.

❓ XATO 8: `what` ni `which/that` bilan adashtirish
The things what you said were interesting.
The things that/which you said were interesting. (yoki "What you said was interesting.")
🔍 Sababi:

what "the thing(s) which" degan ma'noni anglatadi va o'z ichiga otni oladi. Agar gapda "the thing", "the stuff" kabi otlar bo'lsa, ulardan keyin what ishlatilmaydi, that yoki which ishlatiladi.

🔢 XATO 9: Fe'lning sonini moslashtirmaslik
He is one of the students who is always late.
He is one of the students who are always late.
🔍 Sababi:

Relative clause'dagi fe'l (are) o'zidan oldingi olmoshga emas, balki shu olmosh ta'riflayotgan otga moslashishi kerak. Bu yerda "who" "he" ga emas, balki "students" (ko'plik) ga ishora qilmoqda. Shuning uchun fe'l ham ko'plikda bo'lishi kerak.

🤷 XATO 10: `why` o'rniga `which/that` ishlatish
The reason that he failed was obvious.
The reason why he failed was obvious.
🔍 Sababi:

"The reason" so'zidan keyin deyarli har doim sababni ifodalovchi why ishlatiladi. Garchi ba'zi hollarda "that" ishlatilsa ham, "why" tabiiyroq va to'g'riroq hisoblanadi.
