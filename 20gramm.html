Bog'lovchilar Qoidalari

Bog'lovchi (Conjunction) nima?

Gaplarni va gap bo'laklarini bir-biri bilan bog'lash uchun ishlatiladigan so'zlarga bog'lovchilar deyiladi.

1. Teng Bog'lovchilar (Coordinating Conjunctions)

Bu bog'lovchilar teng huquqli gap bo'laklarini yoki gaplarni bog'laydi.

  • But (Lekin/Ammo): Zidlash, qarama-qarshi fikrni bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.
    Ex: The sun has set, but it is still light.
  • And (Va): Fikrlarni qo'shish, ketma-ketlikni bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.
    Ex: She loves to read, and she spends hours in the library.
  • Or (Yoki): Tanlov imkoniyatini bildirish uchun ishlatiladi.
    Ex: You can choose pizza or pasta.

2. Qo'shma Bog'lovchilar (Compound/Subordinating)

Murakkabroq ma'nolarni (maqsad, vaqt, qo'shimcha) ifodalash uchun ishlatiladi.

  • As well as (Ham, shuningdek): "And" ga o'xshash, qo'shimcha ma'lumot qo'shadi.
    Ex: He is a skilled musician, as well as a talented songwriter.
  • So that / In order that (...uchun, maqsadda): Harakatning maqsadini bildiradi.
    Ex: She studied diligently so that she could pass her exam.
  • As soon as (...gach, ...bilanoq): Vaqtni bildiradi, bir ish tugashi bilan ikkinchisi boshlanishini anglatadi.
    Ex: As soon as the snow melts, the hiking trails become accessible.

Bog'lovchilar O'rtasidagi Farqlar

1. And vs. But vs. Or

Eng ko'p ishlatiladigan uchta bog'lovchining asosiy farqi mantiqiy bog'liqlikdadir.

  • And (Qo'shish): Fikrlarni "plyus" (+) qiladi. (A + B)
  • But (Zidlash): Fikrlarni "minus" (-) yoki kutilmagan holatga buradi. (A <-> B)
  • Or (Tanlash): Alternativ taklif qiladi. (A / B)

And: The car is reliable, and it's fuel-efficient. (Ikkala sifat ham bor).

But: The car is fast, but it's expensive. (Tezligi yaxshi, lekin narxi yomon tomoni).

Or: We can visit Paris or Rome. (Faqat bittasini tanlaymiz).

2. So that vs. In order to/that

Maqsadni ifodalashda ishlatiladi, lekin strukturasida farq bo'lishi mumkin.

  • So that: O'zidan keyin to'liq gap (Ega + Kesim) talab qiladi. Ko'pincha can/could/would modal fe'llari bilan keladi.
  • In order that: So that bilan bir xil ma'noda (rasmiyroq), to'liq gap keladi.

So that: He watered the plants so that they would thrive.

In order that: She practiced public speaking in order that she could overcome her fear.

Gap Tuzilmalari (Structures)

But / And / Or (Teng bog'lovchilar)

Gap 1, + but/and/or + Gap 2.

The weather was beautiful, but we forgot to bring sunscreen.

Ob-havo ajoyib edi, lekin biz quyosh kremini olib kelishni unutdik.

He's intelligent, and he's incredibly creative.

U aqlli va u nihoyatda ijodkor.

So that / In order that (Maqsad)

Main Action + so that + S + can/could/would + V1.

They set an early alarm so that they wouldn't miss their flight.

Ular parvozni o'tkazib yubormasliklari uchun erta budilnik qo'yishdi.

He wore protective gear in order that he wouldn't get injured.

U jarohatlanmaslik uchun himoya kiyimini kiyib oldi.

As soon as (Vaqt)

As soon as + Harakat 1, Harakat 2.

As soon as the teacher assigns homework, students should start working on it.

O'qituvchi uy vazifasini berishi bilanoq, talabalar uning ustida ishlashlari kerak.

Rasmlardan Olingan Misollar

BUT (Lekin)

I wanted to buy the shoes, but they didn't have my size.

Men oyoq kiyimlarni sotib olishni xohlagandim, lekin ular mening o'lchamimda emas edi.

The hotel is luxurious, but it's too expensive for us.

Mehmonxona hashamatli, lekin u biz uchun juda qimmat.

He's a talented musician, but he's incredibly shy.

U iqtidorli musiqachi, lekin juda ham uyatchan.

AS WELL AS / OR / AND

The library has a vast collection of books, as well as online resources.

Kutubxona onlayn resurslar kabi katta ko'lamdagi kitoblarga ega.

You can have the blue shirt or the red one.

Siz moviy yoki qizil ko'ylakli egalik qila olasiz.

They are a loving couple, and they have been married for 20 years.

Ular sevishgan juftlik va ular turmush qurishganiga 20 yil bo'ldi.

SO THAT / IN ORDER THAT (Maqsad)

She studied diligently so that she could pass her exam.

U shijoat bilan o'qigan, shuning uchun imtihondan o'ta oldi.

They budgeted their expenses meticulously so that they could save for a new car.

Ular yangi mashina uchun pul tejab qolish maqsadida xarajatlarini e'tibor bilan nazorat qilishdi.

Mashqlar (Conjunctions)

To'g'ri bog'lovchini tanlang

1. I'm tired, ___ I can't go to sleep just yet.

2. They set an early alarm ___ they wouldn't miss their flight.

3. You can choose the blue shirt ___ the red one.

4. She's fluent in English, ___ Spanish and French.

5. ___ the snow melts, the hiking trails become accessible.

6. The restaurant had great reviews, ___ the food was disappointing.

7. They are a loving couple, ___ they have been married for 20 years.

8. She planted flowers in the garden ___ they would bloom beautifully.

9. She's a vegetarian, ___ she occasionally eats fish.

10. ___ the political leaders reach an agreement, it could lead to peace talks.

Bo'sh joyni mos bog'lovchi bilan to'ldiring (and, but, or, so that, as soon as, as well as)

1. The cake looks delicious, ___ I can't have any because of my diet.

2. He exercised every day ___ he could stay in good shape.

3. We can eat at the Italian restaurant ___ the Mexican one.

4. The package includes a laptop, ___ a tablet and a smartphone.

5. ___ the deadline approaches, stress levels tend to rise.

6. He spoke slowly ___ everyone in the audience could understand.

7. The car is reliable, ___ it's also fuel-efficient.

8. He's very intelligent, ___ he lacks common sense.

9. ___ the legal team reviews the case, they will prepare for trial.

10. She packed her umbrella ___ she wouldn't get caught in the rain.

Ikki gapni berilgan bog'lovchi bilan birlashtiring

1. He watered the plants. He wanted them to thrive. → (Use: so that)

2. The car is fast. It is also expensive. → (Use: but)

3. She researched travel destinations. She could plan her vacation. → (Use: in order that)

4. The movie ended. We left the cinema immediately. → (Use: As soon as)

5. He is intelligent. He lacks common sense. → (Use: but)

Eng Ko'p Uchraydigan 10 Ta Xato

🚫 1. `So that` dan keyin fe'lning o'zini ishlatish
I study hard so that pass the exam.
I study hard so that I can pass the exam.

Qoida: `So that` dan keyin har doim yangi ega (Subject) va kesim (Verb) kelishi kerak.

🚫 2. `But` dan oldin vergul qo'ymaslik
The car is fast but it is expensive.
The car is fast, but it is expensive.

Qoida: Agar `but` ikkita to'liq gapni (Ega + Kesim) bog'lasa, undan oldin vergul qo'yiladi.

🚫 3. `As soon as` da kelasi zamon ishlatish
As soon as I will arrive, I will call you.
As soon as I arrive, I will call you.

Qoida: Vaqtni bildiruvchi ergash gaplarda (Time Clauses) kelasi zamon o'rniga hozirgi zamon (Present Simple) ishlatiladi.

🚫 4. `In order to` va `In order that` ni adashtirish
I went there in order that buy bread.
I went there in order to buy bread.

Qoida: `In order TO` dan keyin fe'l (V1) keladi. `In order THAT` dan keyin esa to'liq gap (Ega + Kesim) keladi.

🚫 5. `Although` va `But` ni birga ishlatish
Although it rained, but we went out.
Although it rained, we went out.
It rained, but we went out.

Qoida: Bitta gapda ikkita zidlovchi bog'lovchi ishlatilmaydi. Yo `Although` yo `But` tanlanadi.

🚫 6. `Because` va `So` ni birga ishlatish
Because he was tired, so he went to bed.
Because he was tired, he went to bed.
He was tired, so he went to bed.

Qoida: Sabab (Because) va Natija (So) bog'lovchilari bir vaqtda ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 7. `Both` bilan `Or` ishlatish
I like both coffee or tea.
I like both coffee and tea.

Qoida: `Both` har doim `and` bilan keladi. `Or` esa `Either` bilan ishlatiladi.

🚫 8. `During` dan keyin gap ishlatish
I fell asleep during I was watching TV.
I fell asleep while I was watching TV.
I fell asleep during the movie.

Qoida: `During` dan keyin OT (noun) keladi. `While` dan keyin esa GAP (ega+kesim) keladi.

🚫 9. `Unless` dan keyin inkor ishlatish
I won't go unless you don't come.
I won't go unless you come.

Qoida: `Unless` o'zi "agar ...masa" (if not) degan ma'noni bildiradi, shuning uchun ikkinchi inkor (double negative) kerak emas.

🚫 10. `Despite` dan keyin to'liq gap ishlatish
Despite he is rich, he is unhappy.
Despite being rich, he is unhappy.
Although he is rich, he is unhappy.

Qoida: `Despite` dan keyin ot yoki gerund (V+ing) keladi. Agar to'liq gap kerak bo'lsa, `Although` ishlatiladi.
