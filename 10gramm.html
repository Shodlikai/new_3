Used To, Be Used To & Get Used To - Mukammal Qo'llanma

O'tgan va hozirgi zamondagi odatlar va ko'nikmalarni ifodalash

0%

Asosiy Qoidalar

1. used to + fe'l (O'tgan zamondagi odatlar)

Bu struktura o'tmishda muntazam bajarilgan, ammo hozirda bajarilmaydigan ish-harakatlar yoki holatlar uchun ishlatiladi. Undan keyin har doim fe'lning infinitive shakli (V1) keladi.

  • Ma'nosi: "-ar edi", "-ganman", "-ardi".
  • Faqat o'tgan zamon uchun: Hozirgi zamonda ishlatilmaydi.

I used to play rugby at school. (Men maktabda regbi o'ynardim.) - Hozir o'ynamayman.

He did not use to read a newspaper. (U gazeta o'qimas edi.) - Hozir o'qishi mumkin.

2. be used to + V-ing (Ko'nikkanlik holati)

Bu struktura biror narsaga yoki harakatga ko'nikkanlik, moslashganlik holatini bildiradi. Undan keyin fe'lning -ing (gerund) shakli yoki ot (noun) keladi.

  • Ma'nosi: "-ga o'rganganman", "-ga ko'nikkanman".
  • Hamma zamonlarda: 'be' fe'lini o'zgartirish orqali (am/is/are, was/were, will be) ishlatilishi mumkin.

My friend is used to waking up early. (Mening do'stim erta uyg'onishga o'rgangan.)

She is used to the noisy environment. (U shovqinli muhitga o'rgangan.)

3. get used to + V-ing (Ko'nikish jarayoni)

Bu struktura biror narsaga yoki harakatga ko'nikish, moslashish jarayonini bildiradi. Undan keyin ham -ing (gerund) shakli yoki ot (noun) keladi.

  • Ma'nosi: "-ga o'rganyapman", "-ga ko'nikyapman".
  • Hamma zamonlarda: 'get' fe'lini o'zgartirish orqali (get/gets, got, am getting, will get) ishlatilishi mumkin.

The baby is getting used to sleeping through the night. (Chaqaloq tun bo'yi uxlashga o'rganyapti.)

I can't get used to this cold weather. (Men bu sovuq havoga ko'nika olmayapman.)

Asosiy Farqlar Jadvali

used to vs be used to vs get used to

Bu uchta struktura o'xshash eshitilsa-da, ularning ma'nolari va ishlatilishi mutlaqo farq qiladi.

StrukturaMa'nosiUndan keyin keladiMisol
used to + V1 O'tgan zamondagi odat (endi mavjud emas) Fe'l (infinitive) He used to smoke. (U chekardi.)
be used to + V-ing Ko'nikkanlik holati (normal holat) Fe'l+ing (gerund) yoki Ot (noun) He is used to working hard. (U qattiq ishlashga o'rgangan.)
get used to + V-ing Ko'nikish jarayoni (normal holatga kelish) Fe'l+ing (gerund) yoki Ot (noun) He is getting used to working hard. (U qattiq ishlashga o'rganyapti.)

Gap tuzilmalari

used to - O'tgan zamon odati

S + used to + V1
S + didn't use to + V1
Did + S + use to + V1?

I used to leave my bicycle in the rain.

Velosipedimni yomg'ir ostida qoldirardim.

be used to - Hozirgi ko'nikma

S + am/is/are + used to + V-ing/Noun

My friend is used to waking up early.

Do'stim erta uyg'onishga o'rgangan.

be used to - O'tgan zamon ko'nikmasi

S + was/were + used to + V-ing/Noun

Jessica was used to performing traditional Spanish dances.

Jessika an'anaviy ispan raqslarini ijro etishga o'rgangan edi.

get used to - Ko'nikish jarayoni

S + is/are getting + used to + V-ing/Noun

The baby is getting used to sleeping through the night.

Chaqaloq tun bo'yi uxlashga odatlanyapti.

Misollar bilan tanishing

used to (O'tgan zamon odatlari)

Many people used to use a car to travel.

Ko'p odamlar sayohat qilish uchun mashinadan foydalanishardi.

The whole family used to watch the sunset.

Butun oila quyosh botishini tomosha qilar edi.

Did your mother use to sing to you when you were young?

Yoshligingizda onangiz sizga qo'shiq aytarmidi?

His sister did not use to water the houseplants.

Opasi xona o'simliklarini sug'ormasdi.

be used to (Ko'nikkanlik holati)

The people in big cities are used to heavy traffic.

Katta shaharlardagi odamlar og'ir tirbandlikka o'rganib qolgan.

She is not used to a noisy environment.

U shovqinli muhitga o'rganmagan.

I was not used to managing my time efficiently.

Men vaqtimni samarali boshqarishga o'rganmagan edim.

Are they used to drinking lots of water in hot weather?

Ular issiq havoda ko'p suv ichishga o'rganganmi?

get used to (Ko'nikish jarayoni)

The drivers are getting used to communicating through emails.

Haydovchilar elektron pochta orqali muloqot qilishga o'rganishmoqda.

The girl is getting used to adapting to change.

Bu qiz o'zgarishlarga moslashishga ko'nikmoqda.

You are getting used to participating in team sports.

Siz jamoaviy sport turlarida ishtirok etishga ko'nikyapsiz.

She was not getting used to running a successful business.

U muvaffaqiyatli biznes yuritishga odatlanmayotgan edi.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar

🚫 XATO 1: `be used to` dan keyin V1 ishlatish
I am used to get up early.
I am used to getting up early.

Qoida: `be used to` dan keyin har doim fe'lning `-ing` shakli (gerund) yoki ot keladi.

🚫 XATO 2: `used to` dan keyin V-ing ishlatish
He used to working in a factory.
He used to work in a factory.

Qoida: O'tgan zamon odatlarini bildiruvchi `used to` dan keyin har doim fe'lning oddiy shakli (infinitive) keladi.

🚫 XATO 3: Inkor shakldagi xato
She didn't used to like coffee.
She didn't use to like coffee.

Qoida: Inkor shaklda `did` yordamchi fe'li o'tgan zamonni ko'rsatgani uchun `use` fe'li hozirgi zamon shaklida, ya'ni `-d` qo'shimchasisiz yoziladi.

🚫 XATO 4: So'roq shakldagi xato
Used you to play the piano?
Did you use to play the piano?

Qoida: So'roq shaklda ham `Did` yordamchi fe'li ishlatiladi va `use` fe'li `-d` qo'shimchasisiz yoziladi.

🚫 XATO 5: Hozirgi zamon uchun `use to` ishlatish
I use to wake up at 7 AM every day.
I wake up at 7 AM every day.

Qoida: `used to` faqat o'tgan zamondagi odatlar uchun ishlatiladi. Hozirgi odatlar uchun `Simple Present` (oddiy hozirgi zamon) ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 6: `be` fe'lini unutish
I used to waking up early, so it's not a problem.
I am used to waking up early, so it's not a problem.

Qoida: Ko'nikkanlikni bildirish uchun `used to` dan oldin `be` fe'lining kerakli shakli (am/is/are/was/were) bo'lishi shart.

🚫 XATO 7: Bir martalik harakat uchun `used to`
Yesterday, I used to watch a movie.
Yesterday, I watched a movie.

Qoida: `used to` faqat odat tusiga kirgan, takrorlanib turgan harakatlar uchun ishlatiladi. Bir marta sodir bo'lgan harakat uchun `Simple Past` ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: Bo'lishli gapda `use to` (d harfisiz)
I use to live in London.
I used to live in London.

Qoida: Bo'lishli (daraka) gaplarda har doim `used to` shakli ishlatiladi. `use to` faqat `did` bilan inkor va so'roq gaplarda keladi.

🚫 XATO 9: `be used to` vs. `get used to`
I am still used to the new system. (jarayon)
I am still getting used to the new system.

Qoida: Agar ko'nikish holati hali tugamagan, jarayonda bo'lsa, `get used to` ishlatiladi. `be used to` esa allaqachon ko'nikib bo'lingan holatni bildiradi.

🚫 XATO 10: `to` predlogini unutish
I am used living here.
I am used to living here.

Qoida: `be used to` va `get used to` bir butun ibora hisoblanadi. `to` predlogini tushirib qoldirib bo'lmaydi.
