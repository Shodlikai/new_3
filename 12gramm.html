Majhul Nisbat Qoidalari (Murakkab Zamonlar)

Passive Voice nima va qachon ishlatiladi?

Passive Voice (Majhul nisbat)da gapning fokusi ish-harakatni bajaruvchiga (agent) emas, balki o'sha harakat qaratilgan obyektga tushadi. Ya'ni, ishni kim qilgani emas, nima ish qilingani muhimroq bo'ladi.

Passive Voice Zamonlar Bo'yicha Yasalishi va Ishlatilishi

Present Perfect Passive (has/have been + V3)

Ishlatilishi: O'tgan zamonda noaniq bir vaqtda sodir bo'lgan, ammo natijasi hozirgi kunga ta'sir qiladigan harakatlar uchun. "qilingan", "qilinib bo'ldi" kabi ma'nolarni beradi.

Misol: The book has already been read. (Kitob allaqachon o'qilgan - natijasi hozir ma'lum).

Past Perfect Passive (had been + V3)

Ishlatilishi: O'tgan zamondagi boshqa bir harakatdan oldin sodir bo'lib, tugagan harakat uchun. "qilingan edi", "qilinib bo'lingan edi" ma'nolarini beradi.

Misol: The event had been organized by the time you arrived. (Siz yetib kelguningizcha tadbir tashkillashtirib bo'lingan edi).

Future Perfect Passive (will have been + V3)

Ishlatilishi: Kelajakdagi aniq bir vaqtdan oldin sodir bo'lib, tugallanadigan ish-harakatlar uchun. "qilingan bo'ladi", "qilinib bo'lingan bo'ladi" ma'nolarini beradi.

Misol: The project will have been completed by the end of the week. (Loyiha hafta oxirigacha tugallangan bo'ladi).

Modal Fe'llar + Passive (modal + be + V3)

Ishlatilishi: Modal fe'llar (can, must, should, may, etc.) bilan birga kelib, majburiyat, imkoniyat, tavsiya kabi ma'nolarni majhul nisbatda ifodalaydi.

Misol: The report must be submitted before the deadline. (Hisobot belgilangan muddatdan oldin topshirilishi kerak).

Passive Zamonlar O'rtasidagi Farqlar

1. Past Simple vs. Present Perfect Passive

Asosiy farq harakatning vaqti va hozirgi kunga aloqasidadir.

  • Past Simple Passive (was/were + V3) harakatning o'tmishda aniq bir vaqtda (yesterday, last week) sodir bo'lganini va o'sha yerda qolganini bildiradi.
  • Present Perfect Passive (has/have been + V3) harakatning o'tmishda noaniq bir vaqtda sodir bo'lganini, lekin uning natijasi hozir ko'rinib turganini yoki hozirgi kun uchun ahamiyatli ekanligini bildiradi.

The window was broken yesterday. (Deraza kecha sindirildi - Fokus kechagi voqeada)

The window has been broken. (Deraza sindirilgan - Fokus hozir derazaning singan holatida ekanligida)

2. Present Perfect vs. Past Perfect Passive

Bu ikki zamonning farqi "hozirgacha" va "o'sha paytgacha" degan tushunchalarda yotadi.

  • Present Perfect Passive (has/have been + V3) harakatning hozirgacha bo'lgan vaqt oralig'ida sodir bo'lganini bildiradi.
  • Past Perfect Passive (had been + V3) harakatning o'tmishdagi boshqa bir harakatdan oldinroq bajarilganini bildiradi.

The report has been finished. (Hisobot tugatilgan - hozir u tayyor)

The report had been finished by the time the manager asked for it. (Menejer so'ragan paytgacha hisobot tugatilgan edi)

3. Future Simple vs. Future Perfect Passive

Bu ikki kelasi zamon harakatning qachon tugashiga qarab farqlanadi.

  • Future Simple Passive (will be + V3) harakatning kelajakda biror bir nuqtada bajarilishini aytadi.
  • Future Perfect Passive (will have been + V3) harakatning kelajakdagi aniq bir nuqtadan oldinroq bajarilib bo'lishini bildiradi.

The new bridge will be built next year. (Yangi ko'prik kelasi yili quriladi)

The new bridge will have been built by the end of next year. (Yangi ko'prik kelasi yil oxirigacha qurib bo'linadi)

Gap tuzilmalari (Darak, Inkor, So'roq)

Present Perfect Passive

(+) S + has/have been + V3
(-) S + has/have not been + V3
(?) Has/Have + S + been + V3?

(+) The meal has been cooked by the chef.

Ovqat oshpaz tomonidan pishirildi.

(-) The car has not been repaired yet.

Mashina hali ta'mirlanmagan.

Past Perfect Passive

(+) S + had been + V3
(-) S + had not been + V3
(?) Had + S + been + V3?

(+) The problem had been identified.

Muammo aniqlangan edi.

(-) The coffee had not been brewed.

Qahva qaynatilmagan edi.

Future Perfect Passive

(+) S + will have been + V3
(-) S + will not have been + V3
(?) Will + S + have been + V3?

(+) The report will have been submitted by the deadline.

Hisobot belgilangan muddatgacha topshiriladi.

(?) Will the book have been read by the end of the week?

Kitob hafta oxirigacha o'qib bo'lingan bo'ladimi?

Modal Passive

(+) S + modal + be + V3
(-) S + modal + not + be + V3
(?) Modal + S + be + V3?

Topshiriq talabalar tomonidan yakka tartibda bajarilishi kerak.

(-) The room cannot be reserved online.

Xonani onlayn bron qilish mumkin emas.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Passive Voice - Murakkab)

🚫 XATO 1: Perfect zamonlarda `been` ni unutish
The project has finished by the team.
The project has been finished by the team.

Qoida: Perfect zamonlarning (Present, Past, Future) majhul nisbatida `have/has/had` va `V3` o'rtasida har doim `been` bo'lishi shart.

🚫 XATO 2: Modal fe'llardan keyin `be` ni unutish
The report must submitted.
The report must be submitted.

Qoida: Modal fe'llardan keyingi majhul nisbat formulasi: modal + be + V3. 'be' fe'lini tushirib qoldirib bo'lmaydi.

🚫 XATO 3: `has been` vs. `had been` ni adashtirish
When I arrived, the meeting has already been started.
When I arrived, the meeting had already been started.

Qoida: Agar harakat o'tmishdagi boshqa bir harakatdan oldin sodir bo'lgan bo'lsa (men kelishimdan oldin boshlangan), Past Perfect (`had been`) ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 4: Modal fe'ldan keyin `been` ishlatish
The document should been checked.
The document should be checked.

Qoida: Oddiy modal fe'llardan keyin `be` ishlatiladi. `been` faqat Modal Perfect'larda (should have been) keladi.

🚫 XATO 5: `be` fe'lini unutish (umumiy)
The house built last year.
The house was built last year.

Qoida: Majhul nisbat har doim `be` fe'lining zamonga mos shakli bilan birga keladi. Uni tushirib qoldirish mumkin emas.

🚫 XATO 6: Fe'lning noto'g'ri shakli (V3 o'rniga boshqasi)
The car has been repair.
The car has been repaired.

Qoida: Majhul nisbatda asosiy fe'l har doim 3-shaklda (Past Participle - V3) bo'lishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 7: `by` va `with` ni adashtirish
The door was opened by a key.
The door was opened with a key.

Qoida: Harakatni bajaruvchi shaxs uchun `by` ishlatiladi. Ishni bajarishda ishlatilgan asbob-uskuna uchun `with` ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: So'roq shaklida noto'g'ri so'z tartibi
Has been the problem identified?
Has the problem been identified?

Qoida: So'roq gaplarda birinchi yordamchi fe'l (Has/Have/Had/Will) egadan oldinga o'tadi.

🚫 XATO 9: O'timsiz fe'llarni majhulda ishlatish
A serious accident was happened.
A serious accident happened.

Qoida: `happen`, `arrive`, `die`, `sleep` kabi o'timsiz fe'llar (o'zidan keyin to'ldiruvchi talab qilmaydigan) majhul nisbatda ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 10: `Active` va `Passive`ni aralashtirish
The company was built the bridge.
The company built the bridge. / The bridge was built by the company.

Qoida: Agar gapda harakat bajaruvchisi ega bo'lib kelsa (The company), fe'l Aniq nisbatda bo'lishi kerak. Agar harakat obyekti ega bo'lib kelsa (The bridge), fe'l Majhul nisbatda bo'lishi kerak.
