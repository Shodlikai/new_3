Asosiy Qoidalar

Can

Hozirgi zamondagi qobiliyat, umumiy imkoniyat, ruxsat va norasmiy iltimoslar uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Qobiliyat: I can play the guitar. (Men gitara chala olaman.)
  • Umumiy imkoniyat/haqiqat: It can be very cold here in winter. (Bu yerda qishda juda sovuq bo'lishi mumkin.)
  • Ruxsat: Can I borrow your book? (Kitobingizni olib tursam maylimi?)
  • Iltimos (norasmiy): Can you pass the salt? (Tuzni uzatib yubora olasizmi?)

Could

O'tgan zamondagi umumiy qobiliyat, xushmuomalali iltimos, taklif va ehtimollikni bildiradi.

  • O'tgan qobiliyat (umumiy): I could swim when I was five. (Besh yoshimda suza olardim.)
  • Iltimos (xushmuomalali): Could you please speak more slowly? (Iltimos, sekinroq gapira olasizmi?)
  • Ehtimollik: This could be the house we're looking for. (Bu biz qidirayotgan uy bo'lishi mumkin.)
  • Taklif: We could try that new restaurant. (Ana u yangi restoranni sinab ko'rsak bo'ladi.)

Be able to (va uning kelasi zamon shakli Will/Shall be able to)

be able to - can/could ishlatib bo'lmaydigan barcha holatlar uchun "qutqaruvchi" hisoblanadi.

  • Kelasi zamon:

    will be able to barcha shaxslar uchun ishlatiladi va eng keng tarqalgan shakldir. shall be able to asosan I va we bilan rasmiy yoki British English uslubida ishlatiladi.

    I will be able to help you tomorrow. (Ertaga senga yordam bera olaman.)

    We shall be able to complete the task as scheduled. (Biz vazifani reja bo'yicha bajara olamiz. - rasmiy)

  • Perfect Zamonlar: I haven't been able to finish my work. (Ishimni tugata olmadim.)
  • Modal fe'llardan keyin: You must be able to work on weekends. (Siz dam olish kunlari ishlay olishingiz kerak.)

Would (Iltimoslar uchun)

Would asosan juda xushmuomalali iltimoslar uchun ishlatiladi va bu holatda could ga juda yaqin ma'no beradi.

  • Juda xushmuomalali iltimos: Would you mind closing the door? (Eshikni yopib qo'ysangiz qarshi emasmisiz?)
  • Solishtirma: Could you help me? (Yordam bera olasizmi? - qobiliyatga urg'u). Would you help me? (Yordam berasizmi? - xohishga urg'u). Amalda ikkalasi ham bir xil darajada xushmuomalali hisoblanadi.

Zamonlar va Chuqur Farqlar

Could vs Was/Were able to (O'tgan zamon)

Bu eng muhim va ko'p adashtiriladigan farq. Ikkalasi ham o'tgan zamon, lekin ma'nolari har xil.

TuriQo'llanilishiMisol va Tushuntirish
Could UMUMIY QOBILIYAT. O'tmishda biror ishni qilish qobiliyatiga ega bo'lganingizni bildiradi. Bu qobiliyatdan foydalanib biror natijaga erishganingiz aytilmaydi. My grandfather could speak five languages. (Mening bobom beshta tilda gapira olardi.) - Bu uning umumiy qobiliyati edi.
Was/Were able to ANİQ VAZIYATDAGI YUTUQ. Bir martalik, qiyin vaziyatda biror ishni uddalaganlik, eplaganlik. ("epladi", "uddaladi" deb tarjima qilinadi). The dictionary was in German, but I was able to understand the word. (Lug'at nemis tilida edi, lekin men so'zni tushuna oldim / tushunishni epladim.)

ISTISNO (Eng Muhim Qoida): Inkor shaklda bu farq yo'qoladi. Ikkalasi ham bir xil ma'noni beradi. Odatda couldn't ko'proq ishlatiladi.

I couldn't find my keys. = I wasn't able to find my keys. (Kalitlarimni topa olmadim.)

Iltimoslar: Could you...? vs Would you...?

Ikkalasi ham juda xushmuomalali iltimoslar uchun ishlatiladi va ko'p hollarda bir-birining o'rnida keladi.

StrukturaAsl Ma'nosiMisol
Could you...? "Shuni qilishga qodirmisiz/imkoningiz bormi?" Could you pass me the water? (Suvni uzatib yubora olasizmi?)
Would you...? "Shuni qilishga xohishingiz bormi?" Would you pass me the water? (Suvni uzatib yuborsangiz?)

Xulosa: Kundalik nutqda ikkalasi ham bir xil darajada xushmuomalali hisoblanadi. Sizdan biror narsa so'rashganda "Ha, albatta" deb javob berasiz, "Ha, qodirman" yoki "Ha, xohlayman" deb emas.

Strukturalar

Qobiliyat (Hozirgi/O'tgan)

Ega + can / could + V1

1. She can solve complex problems.

U murakkab muammolarni yecha oladi.

2. I couldn't hear anything.

Men hech narsa eshita olmadim.

Qobiliyat (Kelasi Zamon)

Ega + will/shall be able to + V1

1. He will be able to join us later.

U bizga keyinroq qo'shila oladi.

2. We shall be able to provide a response by Monday. (Rasmiy)

Dushanbagacha javob taqdim eta olamiz.

Iltimos (Polite Requests)

Could/Would + you + V1...?

1. Could you possibly help me?

Iloji bo'lsa, menga yordam bera olasizmi?

2. Would you mind if I opened the window?

Derazani ochsam qarshi emasmisiz?

Infinitive & Modal fe'llar bilan

...to be able to / modal + be able to

1. It's nice to be able to relax on weekends.

Dam olish kunlari dam ola bilish yaxshi.

2. You might be able to get a cheaper ticket online.

Siz onlayn arzonroq chipta ola bilishingiz mumkin.

Misollar To'plami

CAN bilan misollar

I can speak basic Japanese.

Men oddiy yapon tilida gaplasha olaman.

Can you see that bird in the tree?

Daraxtdagi anavi qushni ko'ra olyapsizmi?

You can buy tickets online.

Chiptalarni onlayn sotib olsa bo'ladi.

I'm busy now, but I can call you back in an hour.

Hozir bandman, lekin bir soatdan keyin sizga qayta qo'ng'iroq qila olaman.

She can't come to the phone right now.

U hozir telefonga kela olmaydi.

COULD bilan misollar

He could run a marathon when he was younger.

U yoshroq paytida marafon yugura olardi.

Could you please repeat that?

Iltimos, shuni takrorlay olasizmi?

From my window, I could see the entire city.

Derazamdan butun shaharni ko'ra olardim.

She said she couldn't hear me.

U meni eshita olmayotganini aytdi.

That noise could be a fox.

Bu shovqin tulki bo'lishi mumkin.

WAS/WERE ABLE TO bilan misollar

The firefighters were able to put out the fire before it spread.

O't o'chiruvchilar yong'in tarqalishidan oldin uni o'chira oldilar.

He fell into the river but was able to swim to the side.

U daryoga yiqildi, lekin qirg'oqqa suzib chiqa oldi.

I lost my password, but luckily I was able to reset it.

Parolimni yo'qotib qo'ydim, lekin xayriyatki, uni tiklay oldim.

How were you able to convince him?

Qanday qilib uni ko'ndira oldingiz?

They missed the train, but they were able to catch the next one.

Ular poyezddan qolib ketishdi, lekin keyingisiga ulgura oldilar.

WILL/SHALL BE ABLE TO bilan misollar

Soon, you will be able to speak English without a dictionary.

Tez orada siz lug'atsiz ingliz tilida gapira oladigan bo'lasiz.

I'm sorry, I won't be able to attend the meeting tomorrow.

Kechirasiz, men ertaga yig'ilishda qatnasha olmayman.

Per our agreement, we shall be able to start the project on May 1st.

Kelishuvimizga binoan, biz loyihani 1-mayda boshlay olamiz (rasmiy).

Will you be able to finish this work by Friday?

Jumagacha bu ishni tugata olasizmi?

After this course, I will be able to build my own website.

Bu kursdan so'ng men o'z veb-saytimni yarata oladigan bo'laman.

Muhim Qoidalar va Keng Tarqalgan Xatolar

🚫 XATO 1: "Modal Fe'l + CAN"
You must can swim to join the team.
They will can arrive soon.
You must be able to swim to join the team.
They will be able to arrive soon.
🔍 Sababi:

Ingliz tilining temir qoidasi: ikkita modal fe'l ketma-ket kelmaydi. can modal fe'l bo'lgani uchun must, will, should, may, might kabi boshqa modal fe'llardan keyin kela olmaydi. Bu holatlarda har doim be able to ishlatiladi.

❌ XATO 2: `can` fe'lining Infinitive shakli
I hope to can visit you next summer.
I hope to be able to visit you next summer.
🔍 Sababi:

can modal fe'l bo'lgani uchun uning to bilan boshlanadigan infinitive shakli yo'q. Bu holatlarda ham be able to yordamga keladi (to be able to).

🤔 XATO 3: Umumiy qobiliyat va aniq yutuqni adashtirish
Vaziyat: Kecha imtihon juda qiyin edi, lekin men barcha savollarga javob berdim.
The exam was difficult, but I could answer all the questions.
The exam was difficult, but I was able to answer all the questions.
🔍 Sababi:

Bu yerda gap sizning umumiy bilimingiz haqida emas, balki aniq bir qiyin vaziyatni uddalaganingiz haqida ketyapti. Bunday "epladim", "uddaladim" ma'nosini was/were able to beradi. I could answer "javob bera olardim" degan umumiy ma'noni berib qoladi.

❓ XATO 4: Ruxsat va Iltimosni adashtirish
Siz do'stingizdan eshikni yopishini so'ramoqchisiz.
Can I close the door?
Can you close the door? / Could you close the door?
🔍 Sababi:

Can I...? / Could I...? - O'ZINGIZ biror ish qilish uchun ruxsat so'rash. ("Men yopsam maylimi?").

Can you...? / Could you...? / Would you...? - BOSHQA odamdan biror ish qilishni so'rash (iltimos). ("Siz yopa olasizmi?")

💡 "Oltin Qoidalar" To'plami
1. Gap kelasi zamon, perfect zamon yoki infinitive haqida bo'lsa -> BE ABLE TO.
2. Gapda boshqa modal fe'l (must, should...) bo'lsa -> BE ABLE TO.
3. O'tgan zamonda "epladim", "uddaladim" ma'nosi bo'lsa -> WAS/WERE ABLE TO.
4. O'tgan zamonda umumiy qobiliyat ("qila olardim") bo'lsa -> COULD.
5. O'tgan zamonda inkor bo'lsa, farqi yo'q -> COULDN'T (odatda).
6. Xushmuomalali iltimos -> COULD you...? yoki WOULD you...?
