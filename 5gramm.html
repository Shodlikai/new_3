Ravishlar (Adverbs) Qoidalari

Ravish nima va uning vazifasi

Sifat (adjective) otni (noun) tasvirlasa, ravish (adverb) asosan fe'lni (verb) tasvirlaydi. U harakatning qanday, qayerda, qachon yoki qanchalik tez-tez sodir bo'lishini ko'rsatadi. Shuningdek, ravishlar sifatlarni va boshqa ravishlarni ham tasvirlashi mumkin.

  • Fe'lni tasvirlaydi: He walks slowly. (U sekin yuradi.)
  • Sifatni tasvirlaydi: She is very beautiful. (U juda chiroyli.)
  • Boshqa ravishni tasvirlaydi: He walks too slowly. (U juda ham sekin yuradi.)

Ravish yasalishi: -ly qo'shimchasi

Ko'pgina ravishlar sifatga -ly qo'shimchasini qo'shish orqali yasaladi.

Imlo qoidalari:

  • Agar sifat -y bilan tugasa, y harfi i ga o'zgaradi va -ly qo'shiladi: happy → happily.
  • Agar sifat -le bilan tugasa, e harfi tushib qolib, -y qo'shiladi: possible → possibly.
  • Agar sifat -ic bilan tugasa, odatda -ally qo'shiladi: dramatic → dramatically.

Sifat va Ravish shakli bir xil so'zlar

Ba'zi so'zlarning sifat va ravish shakli bir xil bo'ladi va ularga -ly qo'shilmaydi. Eng ko'p uchraydiganlari: fast, hard, early, late, high, low, straight.

This is a fast car. (sifat) / He drives fast. (ravish)

It was a hard exam. (sifat) / She works hard. (ravish)

Ravishlarning darajalari (Degrees of Adverbs)

Sifatlar kabi, ravishlar ham 3 ta darajaga ega: oddiy (positive), qiyosiy (comparative) va orttirma (superlative).

  • Qiyosiy daraja: Ikki ish-harakatni solishtirish uchun ishlatiladi. Ko'pincha more ... than yoki -er ... than bilan yasaladi: He runs faster than me. She speaks more slowly than her sister.
  • Orttirma daraja: Bir ish-harakatni boshqa barcha shu turdagi harakatlar bilan solishtirish uchun ishlatiladi. Ko'pincha the most ... yoki the -est bilan yasaladi: He runs the fastest. She speaks the most slowly of all.
  • Tenglik: as ... as strukturasi yordamida harakatlarning tengligini ko'rsatish mumkin: He speaks English as well as his teacher.

Ma'nosi o'zgaradigan ravishlar

Ba'zi ravishlar -ly qo'shimchasini olganda ma'nosi butunlay o'zgaradi.

So'zRavish (ma'no 1)Ravish + ly (ma'no 2)
Hard qattiq, ko'p (She works hard.) Hardly - zo'rg'a, deyarli... emas (I can hardly see.)
Late kech (He arrived late.) Lately - yaqinda, so'nggi paytlarda (Have you seen him lately?)

Ravishlar Strukturalari

Fe'l + Ravish

Ega + Fe'l + Ravish

He walks slowly.

U sekin yuradi.

She speaks English fairly well.

U ingliz tilida ancha yaxshi gapiradi.

Ravish + Sifat

... Ravish + Sifat

Qiyosiy Daraja (Comparative)

... ravish-er / more ravish + than ...

A black horse runs faster than a white horse.

Qora ot oq otdan tezroq yuguradi.

Bobby speaks more clearly than his friends.

Bobby do'stlaridan ko'ra aniqroq gapiradi.

Orttirma Daraja (Superlative)

... the ravish-est / most ravish ...

Tom goes the slowest.

Tom eng sekin boradi.

Michelle smiled the most happily.

Michelle eng baxtiyor tabassum qildi.

Tenglik darajasi (as...as)

... as ravish as ...

Do they speak English as well as their teachers?

Ular o'qituvchilari kabi ingliz tilida yaxshi gapirishadimi?

That star is shining as brightly as the sun.

Ana bu yulduz quyoshdek yorqin porlayapti.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Ravishlar)

🚫 XATO 1: Sifat o'rnida ravish ishlatish
He drives very slow.
He drives very slowly.

Qoida: Fe'lni ('drives') tasvirlash uchun sifat ('slow') emas, ravish ('slowly') ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 2: 'good' va 'well'ni adashtirish
She speaks English good.
She speaks English well.

Qoida: 'Good' - sifat (a good student). 'Well' - ravish (she speaks well). Ish-harakatni tasvirlash uchun 'well' ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 3: 'hard' va 'hardly'ni adashtirish
He works very hardly.
He works very hard.

Qoida: 'Hard' - "qattiq, ko'p" (ishlash). 'Hardly' - "zo'rg'a, deyarli...emas" (I can hardly see).

🚫 XATO 4: 'late' va 'lately'ni adashtirish
I haven't seen him late.
I haven't seen him lately.

Qoida: 'Late' - "kech, kechqurun". 'Lately' - "yaqinda, so'nggi paytlarda".

🚫 XATO 5: Orttirma darajada xato
He runs more fast than me.
He runs faster than me.

Qoida: 'Fast' kabi bir bo'g'inli ravishlarga 'more' emas, '-er' qo'shimchasi qo'shiladi.

🚫 XATO 6: 'Fastly' so'zini ishlatish
She finished the race fastly.
She finished the race fast.

Qoida: 'Fastly' degan so'z ingliz tilida mavjud emas. 'Fast' so'zining o'zi ham sifat, ham ravish bo'lib kela oladi.

🚫 XATO 7: Holat fe'llaridan keyin ravish
The food smells deliciously.
The food smells delicious.

Qoida: 'smell', 'taste', 'feel', 'look', 'seem' kabi holat fe'llaridan keyin odatda ravish emas, sifat ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: Qiyosiy darajada qo'sh xato
She sings more better than him.
She sings better than him.

Qoida: 'Better' allaqachon 'well'ning qiyosiy darajasi hisoblanadi. Uning oldidan 'more' ishlatish ortiqcha va xato.

🚫 XATO 9: 'friendly', 'lonely' kabi so'zlardan ravish yasash
He greeted me friendly.
He greeted me in a friendly way.

Qoida: 'Friendly', 'lonely', 'lovely' kabi -ly bilan tugaydigan so'zlar sifatlardir. Ulardan ravish yasash uchun "in a ... way/manner" iborasi ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 10: 'hardly' bilan qo'sh inkor
I can't hardly hear you.
I can hardly hear you.

Qoida: 'Hardly' so'zining o'zi inkor ma'nosini beradi ("deyarli...emas"). Shuning uchun undan oldin 'not' (can't, don't) ishlatilmaydi.
