Non-Defining Relative Clause Qoidalari

Non-Defining Clause nima?

Ba'zida otga (shaxs yoki narsaga) qo'shimcha ma'lumot berish uchun ergash gap ishlatiladi. Bu ma'lumot gapning asosiy ma'nosi uchun shart bo'lmaydi, shunchaki izoh beradi. Bunday ergash gaplar 'non-defining relative clause' deb ataladi va ular har doim vergullar bilan ajratiladi.

Mike, who is planting flowers, is 19 years old. (Mayk, gullarni sug'orayotgan bola, 19 yoshda.)

Bu yerda "Mike" kimligi bizga ma'lum, ergash gap esa u haqida shunchaki qo'shimcha ma'lumot bermoqda.

Jonsiz buyumlar uchun: faqat which

Agar ta'riflanayotgan ot jonsiz buyum bo'lsa, non-defining ergash gapda faqat which ishlatiladi. Bu holatda that ishlatib bo'lmaydi va whichni tushirib qoldirib ham bo'lmaydi.

The car, which is blue, is mine. (Moviy rangdagi mashina meniki.)

The car, that is blue, is mine. (XATO)

To'ldiruvchi uchun: faqat whom

Non-defining ergash gapda ta'riflanayotgan ot ergash gapga nisbatan to'ldiruvchi bo'lsa, faqat whom ishlatiladi. Bu holatda who ishlatish yoki whomni tushirib qoldirish mumkin emas.

The teacher, whom he likes, will retire next year. (U yaxshi ko'radigan o'qituvchi kelasi yili nafaqaga chiqadi.)

The teacher, who he likes, will retire... (XATO)

Egalik uchun: whose

Non-defining ergash gapda egalikni ifodalash uchun whose ishlatiladi. Uni boshqa olmosh bilan almashtirib yoki tushirib qoldirib bo'lmaydi.

Sheila, whose favorite color is blue, has just moved to Washington. (Yoqtirgan rangi ko'k bo'lgan Sheila endigina Vashingtonga ko'chdi.)

Non-Defining Clause Strukturalari

, who ... , (Shaxslar uchun)

Noun, + who + Verb..., ...

Mike, who is planting flowers, is 19 years old.

Gullarni sug'orayotgan Mayk 19 yoshda.

, which ... , (Narsalar uchun)

Noun, + which + Verb..., ...

The Eiffel Tower, which is located in Paris, is a famous landmark.

Parijda joylashgan Eyfel minorasi mashhur diqqatga sazovor joy.

, whom ... , (To'ldiruvchi shaxs)

Noun, + whom + S + V..., ...

The teacher, whom he likes, will retire next year.

U yaxshi ko'radigan o'qituvchi kelasi yili iste'foga chiqadi.

, whose ... , (Egalik)

Noun, + whose + Noun..., ...

Robert, whose mother became ill yesterday, studies in Tokyo.

Onasi kecha kasal bo'lib qolgan Robert Tokyoda o'qiydi.

Non-Defining Clause Misollari

Shaxslar uchun (Who, Whom, Whose)

John's mother, who lives in Scotland, has 6 grandchildren.

Shotlandiyada yashaydigan Jonning onasini 6 ta nabirasi bor.

The person, whom you respect, is departing tomorrow.

Siz hurmat qiladigan inson ertaga jo'nab ketyapti.

Robert, whose mother became ill yesterday, studies in Tokyo.

Onasi kecha kasal bo'lib qolgan Robert Tokyoda o'qiydi.

Narsalar va Joylar uchun (Which, Where)

I will go to the restaurant, which serves Italian food.

Men italyan taomini taklif qiladigan restoranga boraman.

Is the book, which you borrowed from the library, interesting?

Siz kutubxonadan qarzga olgan kitob qiziqarlimi?

Paris, where the Eiffel Tower is, is a beautiful city.

Eyfel minorasi joylashgan Parij go'zal shahar.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Non-Defining Clauses)

🚫 XATO 1: Vergullarni unutish
My brother who lives in London is a doctor.
My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor.

Qoida: Non-defining clause har doim bosh va oxiridan vergul bilan ajratilishi shart.

🚫 XATO 2: 'that' ishlatish
My car, that is very old, often breaks down.
My car, which is very old, often breaks down.

Qoida: Vergullar bilan ajratilgan (non-defining) ergash gaplarda that mutlaqo ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 3: To'ldiruvchi uchun 'who' ishlatish
The manager, who I spoke to, was helpful.
The manager, whom I spoke to, was helpful.

Qoida: Non-defining clause'da olmosh to'ldiruvchi ('I spoke to him') vazifasida kelsa, 'who' emas, balki 'whom' ishlatilishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 4: Olmoshni tushirib qoldirish
My sister, lives in Tashkent, is a student.
My sister, who lives in Tashkent, is a student.

Qoida: Non-defining clause'larda nisbiy olmoshni (who, which, whom, whose) hech qachon tushirib qoldirib bo'lmaydi.

🚫 XATO 5: Whose vs Who's
That's the woman, who's husband is a famous actor.
That's the woman, whose husband is a famous actor.

Qoida: WHOSE egalikni bildiradi ("-ning"). WHO'S esa "Who is" ning qisqartmasi.

🚫 XATO 6: Ortiqcha olmosh ishlatish
This is the book, which I bought it yesterday.
This is the book, which I bought yesterday.

Qoida: Nisbiy olmosh (which) allaqachon to'ldiruvchi vazifasini bajarayotgani uchun, gapda yana bir marta to'ldiruvchi olmoshni (it) ishlatish shart emas.

🚫 XATO 7: 'what' va 'which'ni adashtirish
I read the book "The Old Man and the Sea", what you recommended.
I read the book "The Old Man and the Sea", which you recommended.

Qoida: what o'zidan oldingi otni aniqlash uchun ishlatilmaydi. Otdan keyin which ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: Ega-kesim moslashuvi xatosi
My friends, who is visiting from abroad, are staying with me.
My friends, who are visiting from abroad, are staying with me.

Qoida: Ergash gapdagi fe'l who/which olmoshi aniqlayotgan otga moslashishi kerak. 'who' bu yerda 'friends' (ko'plik) ga ishora qilmoqda, shuning uchun fe'l ham ko'plikda (are) bo'lishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 9: Shaxs uchun 'which' ishlatish
My neighbor, which is a mechanic, fixed my car.
My neighbor, who is a mechanic, fixed my car.

Qoida: Shaxslarga nisbatan qo'shimcha ma'lumot berilganda 'who' ishlatiladi, 'which' emas.

🚫 XATO 10: Faqat bitta vergul ishlatish
The president, who gave a long speech was tired.
The president, who gave a long speech, was tired.

Qoida: Agar non-defining clause gapning o'rtasida kelsa, u ham oldidan, ham oxiridan vergul bilan ajratilishi shart.
