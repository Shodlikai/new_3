"Have Something Done" Qoidalari

Asosiy G'oya Nima?

Bu qolip biror ish-harakatni o'zimiz emas, balki boshqa bir kishiga (odatda professionalga) pul evaziga yoki iltimos qilib bajartirganimizda ishlatiladi.

  • Ma'nosi: Ishni o'zim qilmadim, kimdir men uchun qildi.
  • Fokus: Natijaga, ish-harakatning o'ziga e'tibor qaratiladi, uni kim bajargani muhim emas.

I had my hair cut. (Men sochimni oldirdim.) - Ya'ni, sartarosh sochimni oldi.

She had her car repaired. (U mashinasini tuzattirdi.) - Ya'ni, usta mashinasini tuzatdi.

Asosiy Formula

Bu strukturani yasash juda oson va u deyarli barcha zamonlarda bir xil qolipga tushadi:

Ega + HAVE (zamonlarda o'zgaradi) + Obyekt + Fe'l 3 (V3)

I have my house cleaned every month. (Men har oy uyimni tozalattiraman.)

We had the documents delivered yesterday. (Biz kecha hujjatlarni yetkaztirib berdik.)

Have vs Get

Have something done o'rniga ba'zan get something done ham ishlatiladi. Ma'nosi deyarli bir xil, ammo kichik farqi bor.

  • Have something done: Neytral va standart forma. Yozma va og'zaki nutqda keng qo'llaniladi.
  • Get something done: Ko'proq og'zaki nutqda ishlatiladigan norasmiy (informal) shakl.

I need to have my watch repaired. (Standart)

I need to get my watch repaired. (Norasmiy)

Salbiy Hodisalar Uchun Ishlatilishi

Bu qolip faqat xizmatlar uchun emas, balki biz bilan sodir bo'lgan yoqimsiz, salbiy hodisalarni aytish uchun ham ishlatiladi.

He had his wallet stolen on the bus. (Avtobusda uning hamyonini o'g'irlatib qo'yishdi.)

She had her arm broken while skiing. (U chang'i uchayotganda qo'lini sindirib oldi.)

Bu yerda ham ishni o'zi bajarmagani, balki boshqa birov yoki vaziyat sababli shu holatga tushgani nazarda tutiladi.

Zamonlar va Farqlar

Active vs. Causative ("O'zi qildi" vs "Qildirdi")

Eng asosiy farq - ishni kim bajarganida.

TuriGapMa'nosi
Active I painted my room. Men xonamni o'zim bo'yadim. Bo'yoqchi menman.
Causative I had my room painted. Men xonamni bo'yattirdim. Boshqa odam (bo'yoqchi) men uchun bo'yadi.
Active She fixed the computer. U kompyuterni o'zi tuzatdi.
Causative She had the computer fixed. U kompyuterni tuzattirdi (ustaga).

"Have something done" vs "Have someone do something"

Bu ikki struktura o'xshash, lekin fokusi har xil.

StrukturaMisolFokus (e'tibor)
Have + Obyekt + V3 I had the car repaired. Natijaga (mashina tuzatildi). Kim tuzatgani muhim emas.
Have + Bajaruvchi + V1 I had the mechanic repair the car. Bajaruvchiga (usta tuzatdi). Kim qilgani aytib o'tilgan.

Turli Zamonlarda Qo'llanilishi

Bu qolipdagi have fe'li zamonlarga qarab o'zgaradi. V3 esa hech qachon o'zgarmaydi!

ZamonStrukturaMisol
Present Simple have/has + object + V3 She has her car serviced every year.
Past Simple had + object + V3 I had my photo taken yesterday.
Future Simple will have + object + V3 We will have the goods delivered next week.
Present Perfect have/has had + object + V3 She has just had her hair done.
Past Perfect had had + object + V3 He had had his suit cleaned before the party.
Present Continuous am/is/are having + object + V3 I'm having a new window installed right now.
Past Continuous was/were having + object + V3 They were having their house painted when I called.
'be going to' be going to have + object + V3 I'm going to have my eyes checked.

"Have Something Done" Strukturalari

Present Simple

Ega + have/has + obyekt + V3

1. I have my car washed every week.

Men har hafta mashinamni yuvdiraman.

2. She has her nails done twice a month.

U oyiga ikki marta tirnoqlarini qildiradi.

Past Simple

Ega + had + obyekt + V3

1. We had our house painted last year.

Biz o'tgan yili uyimizni bo'yattirdik.

2. He had his passport stolen.

U pasportini o'g'irlatib qo'ydi.

Future Simple

Ega + will have + obyekt + V3

1. I will have this letter sent tomorrow.

Men ertaga bu xatni jo'nattiraman.

2. They will have the report finished by Friday.

Ular juma kunigacha hisobotni tugattirishadi.

Present Perfect

Ega + have/has had + obyekt + V3

1. I've just had my hair cut.

Men hozirgina sochimni oldirdim.

2. She has had two books published so far.

U hozirgacha ikkita kitobini nashr ettirdi.

Present Continuous

Ega + am/is/are having + obyekt + V3

1. Sorry for the noise, we are having a new AC installed.

Shovqin uchun uzr, biz yangi konditsioner o'rnattiryapmiz.

2. She is having her portrait painted.

U o'z portretini chizdiryapti.

Modals bilan

Ega + modal + have + obyekt + V3

1. You should have your eyes tested.

Siz ko'zlaringizni tekshirtirishingiz kerak.

2. We must have this problem solved immediately.

Biz bu muammoni zudlik bilan hal qildirishimiz shart.

So'roq Shakli

Do/Did/Will + ega + have + obyekt + V3?

1. Did you have your car repaired?

Mashinangizni tuzattirdingizmi?

2. Where do you usually have your hair cut?

Odatda sochingizni qayerda oldirasiz?

Inkor Shakli

Ega + don't/didn't + have + obyekt + V3

1. I didn't have the package delivered on time.

Men jo'natmani o'z vaqtida yetkaztira olmadim.

2. She doesn't have her house cleaned by anyone.

U uyini hech kimga tozalattirmaydi (o'zi tozalaydi).

"Have Something Done" Misollari

Xizmatlar Uchun

I'm going to have my suit cleaned for the wedding.

Men to'y uchun kostyumimni tozalattirmoqchiman.

She has her groceries delivered every Friday.

U har juma oziq-ovqat mahsulotlarini yetkaztirib beradi.

We need to have our passports renewed.

Biz pasportlarimizni yangilatishimiz kerak.

He is famous. He has his biography written by a professional writer.

U mashhur. U o'z tarjimai holini professional yozuvchiga yozdirgan.

Salbiy Hodisalar Uchun

My friend had his phone stolen at the concert.

Do'stimning telefonini konsertda o'g'irlatib qo'yishdi.

How did you have your leg broken?

Qanday qilib oyog'ingizni sindirib oldingiz?

The politician had his reputation damaged by the scandal.

Siyosatchining obro'si janjal tufayli putur yetkazildi.

We had our flight cancelled due to bad weather.

Yomon ob-havo tufayli bizning reysimiz bekor qilindi.

"GET" bilan (Norasmiy)

I really need to get my hair cut. It's too long.

Sochimni oldirishim kerak. Juda uzun bo'lib ketdi.

Can we get this pizza delivered to our address?

Shu pitsani manzilimizga yetkazib bera olamizmi?

He finally got his car fixed.

U nihoyat mashinasini tuzattirdi.

Mashqlar (5 xil turdagi)

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar

🔄 XATO 1: Active va Causative ma'nolarini adashtirish
I am a hairdresser. Yesterday, I had my hair cut.
Men sartaroshman. Kecha men sochimni oldirdim. (Bu gapda ma'no xatosi bor)
I am a hairdresser. Yesterday, I cut my hair.
Men sartaroshman. Kecha men sochimni oldim. (O'zi kesgan)
🔍 Tushuntirish:

Agar ishni o'zingiz bajarsangiz, oddiy aktiv gap ishlatasiz (I cut my hair). Agar kimdir siz uchun bajarsa, causative ishlatasiz (I had my hair cut).

🔢 XATO 2: Noto'g'ri fe'l shaklini ishlatish
She had her car repair.
She had her car repaired.
🔍 Tushuntirish:

Bu qolipda DOIM fe'lning 3-shakli, ya'ni Past Participle (V3) ishlatiladi. 'have' fe'lining zamoni o'zgarsa ham, V3 o'zgarmaydi.

📍 XATO 3: So'z tartibini buzish
I am having installed a new window.
I am having a new window installed.
🔍 Tushuntirish:

Obyekt (ish bajarilayotgan narsa) doim have va V3 ning o'rtasida keladi.

Formula: ...have + OBYEKT + V3

⏰ XATO 4: Zamonni noto'g'ri o'zgartirish
I have my car repaired yesterday.
I had my car repaired yesterday.
🔍 Tushuntirish:

Zamonni ifodalash uchun asosiy fe'l (V3) emas, balki 'have' fe'li o'zgartiriladi. 'yesterday' bo'lsa, 'have' -> 'had' ga aylanadi.

💡 ESDA SAQLANG
Bu qolipni ishlatish uchun o'zingizga savol bering:
"Ishni o'zim qildimmi yoki kimdir men uchun qildimi?"
Tez tekshirish usuli:

1. Zamonni aniqlang (now, yesterday, every day...)

2. Shu zamonga qarab 'have' ni o'zgartiring (am having, had, have/has...)

3. Obyektni qo'ying.

4. Asosiy fe'lni V3 shaklida qo'shing.
