Istak-Mayl (Wish) Qoidalari

WISH + Past Simple (Hozirgi zamon afsusi)

Ishlatilishi: Hozirgi zamondagi noreal, biz o'zgartirishni xohlaydigan vaziyatlar haqida afsuslanish yoki istak bildirish uchun ishlatiladi. Gap o'tgan zamonda tuzilsa ham, ma'nosi hozirgi zamonga tegishli bo'ladi.

Ma'nosi: "Qaniydi hozir shunday bo'lsa/bo'lmasa".

I wish I knew English. (Qaniydi, men ingliz tilini bilganimda. - Lekin bilmayman)

I wish it weren't snowing now. (Qaniydi, hozir qor yog'mayotgan bo'lganda. - Lekin yoqyapti)

Muhim: Bu strukturada I/he/she/it uchun ham `was` o'rniga `were` ishlatilishi odatiy holdir: I wish I were a doctor.

WISH + Past Perfect (O'tgan zamon afsusi)

Ishlatilishi: O'tgan zamonda sodir bo'lgan yoki bo'lmagan ish-harakatlar haqida afsuslanish uchun ishlatiladi. Bu o'zgartirib bo'lmaydigan o'tmish haqidagi afsusdir.

Ma'nosi: "Qaniydi o'shanda shunday qilganimda/qilmaganimda".

I wish I had studied harder at school. (Qaniydi, maktabda qattiqroq o'qiganimda. - Lekin qattiq o'qimaganman)

I wish I hadn't eaten at the Chinese restaurant. (Qaniydi, Xitoy restoranida ovqatlanmaganimda)

WISH + would (Shikoyat va norozilik)

Ishlatilishi: Boshqa biror shaxsning takrorlanadigan, bizga yoqmaydigan odati yoki harakatidan norozilikni, shikoyatni bildirish uchun ishlatiladi. Biz bu vaziyat o'zgarishini xohlaymiz.

Ma'nosi: "Qaniydi, ...ni bas qilsa/qilsa edi".

I wish you would put those shelves up soon. (Qaniydi, siz anavi tokchalarni devorga tezda o'rnatsangiz)

Tom wishes his neighbours wouldn't make so much noise. (Tom qo'shnilari bunchalik shovqin solmasligini xohlaydi)

WISH + could (Qobiliyatsizlikdan afsuslanish)

Ishlatilishi: Hozirgi yoki kelajakda biror ishni bajara olmaslikdan (qobiliyat yoki imkoniyat yo'qligidan) afsuslanishni bildiradi.

Ma'nosi: "Qaniydi, ... qila olsam".

I wish I could drive. (Qaniydi, mashina haydashni bilsam)

I wish I could go to the party. (Qaniydi, ziyofatga bora olsam)

`WISH` Strukturalari O'rtasidagi Farqlar

1. Hozirgi Afsus (Wish + Past Simple) vs. O'tgan Zamondagi Afsus (Wish + Past Perfect)

Asosiy farq — afsuslanayotgan vaziyatning **qaysi zamonga tegishli** ekanligida.

  • Wish + Past Simple: Afsus **hozirgi** vaziyat haqida. Gapda `now`, `today` kabi so'zlar bo'lishi yoki kontekstdan hozirgi zamon tushunilishi kerak.
  • Wish + Past Perfect: Afsus **o'tgan** zamondagi vaziyat haqida. Gapda `yesterday`, `last year` kabi so'zlar bo'lishi yoki kontekstdan o'tgan zamon tushunilishi kerak.

I wish I knew his name. (Qaniydi, uning ismini bilsam - **hozir** bilmayman).

I wish I had known his name at the party yesterday. (Qaniydi, kecha ziyofatda uning ismini bilganimda - **kecha** bilmaganman).

2. Shikoyat (Wish + would) vs. Qobiliyatsizlik (Wish + could)

Bu ikki struktura ham istakni bildiradi, lekin urg'u har xil narsaga qaratiladi.

  • Wish + would: Siz **boshqa birovning harakatidan** norozisiz va u o'zgarishini xohlaysiz. Bu shikoyatga yaqin.
  • Wish + could: Siz **o'zingizning qobiliyatsizligingizdan** afsusdasiz. Bu imkoniyat yo'qligidan afsuslanish.

I wish my brother would stop smoking. (Qaniydi, akam chekishni tashlasa - Men uning harakatidan noroziman).

I wish I could stop smoking. (Qaniydi, chekishni tashlay olsam - Men o'zimning qobiliyatsizligimdan afsusdaman).

Istak-Mayl Gap Tuzilmalari

Hozirgi Zamon Istagi/Afsusi

I wish + S + Past Simple (V2/ed).

I wish I knew English.

Qaniydi, men ingliz tilini bilganimda.

I wish I were a doctor.

Qaniydi, men doktor bo'lganimda.

O'tgan Zamon Afsusi

I wish + S + Past Perfect (had + V3).

I wish I had studied harder at school.

Qaniydi, maktabda qattiqroq o'qiganimda.

I wish I hadn't eaten at the Chinese restaurant.

Qaniydi, Xitoy restoranida ovqatlanmaganimda.

Shikoyat / Norozilik

I wish + S + would(n't) + V1.

I wish you would obey this rule.

Qaniydi, siz bu qoidaga bo'ysunsangiz.

Tom wishes his neighbours wouldn't make so much noise.

Tom qo'shnilari bunchalik shovqin solmasligini xohlaydi.

Qobiliyatsizlikdan Afsuslanish

I wish + S + could(n't) + V1.

I wish I could drive.

Qaniydi, mashina boshqara olganimda.

I wish I could go to the party.

Qaniydi, ziyofatga bora olsam.

Misollar bilan tanishing

Hozirgi zamon afsusi (wish + Past Simple)

I wish I had a fast car.

Qaniydi, menda tezkor mashina bo'lganida.

I wish I were a teacher.

Qaniydi, men o'qituvchi bo'lganimda.

O'tgan zamon afsusi (wish + Past Perfect)

I wish I hadn't eaten so much. (Men juda ko'p yeb qo'yganimdan afsusdaman)

Qaniydi, buncha ko'p yemaganimda.

I wish I hadn't gone to Thailand.

Qaniydi, Tailandga bormaganimda.

Shikoyat (wish + would)

I wish she wouldn't write this letter.

Qaniydi, u bu xatni yozmasa.

I wish you would stop making that noise. (Jahlimni chiqarayotgan shovqinni to'xtatishingni xohlayman)

Qaniydi, shu shovqinni to'xtatsangiz.

Qobiliyatsizlikdan afsus (wish + could)

I feel so helpless. I wish I could speak this language.

O'zimni juda noiloj his qilyapman. Qaniydi, men shu tilda gapira olsam.

I wish I could play the piano.

Qaniydi, pianino chala olganimda.

Mashqlar (WISH Clauses)

To'g'ri variantni tanlang

1. I don't have a car. I wish I ___ a car.

2. I ate too much yesterday. I wish I ___ so much.

3. My neighbors are so noisy. I wish they ___ so much noise.

4. I can't speak Japanese. I wish I ___ speak it.

5. I'm not rich. I wish I ___ rich.

6. I didn't go to the party last week. I wish I ___.

7. It's raining. I wish it ___ raining.

8. I couldn't help you yesterday. I wish I ___ you.

9. This problem is difficult. I wish it ___ so difficult.

10. I said something stupid. I wish I ___ that.

Bo'sh joyni fe'lning to'g'ri shakli bilan to'ldiring.

1. It's so cold today. I wish it ___ warmer. (be)

2. I missed my flight. I wish I ___ earlier. (leave)

3. My car is very old. I wish I ___ a new one. (have)

4. You are making too much noise. I wish you ___ quiet. (be)

5. I'm sorry I can't help you. I wish I ___ help you. (can)

6. He failed the test. He wishes he ___ harder. (study)

7. I don't know what to do. I wish I ___ what to do. (know)

8. The weather was bad yesterday. I wish it ___ so bad. (not/be)

9. The bus is late again. I wish it ___ on time. (come)

10. I can't afford that dress. I wish I ___ afford it. (can)

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (WISH)

🚫 XATO 1: Hozirgi zamon istagi uchun hozirgi zamonni ishlatish
I wish I have a new car.
I wish I had a new car.

Qoida: Hozirgi zamon haqidagi noreal istak/afsus uchun `wish` dan keyin har doim **Past Simple** (o'tgan oddiy zamon) ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 2: O'tgan zamon afsusi uchun Past Simple ishlatish
I wish I didn't eat so much yesterday.
I wish I hadn't eaten so much yesterday.

Qoida: O'tgan zamon haqidagi afsuslar uchun har doim **Past Perfect** (`had + V3`) ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 3: Shikoyat uchun `will` ishlatish
I wish you will stop making noise.
I wish you would stop making noise.

Qoida: Boshqa birovning harakatidan norozilikni yoki o'zgarishini xohlayotganimizni bildirish uchun `wish` dan keyin **would** ishlatiladi, `will` emas.

🚫 XATO 4: O'z-o'ziga shikoyat qilish (`I wish I would`)
I wish I would stop smoking.
I wish I could stop smoking.

Qoida: Odam o'z-o'ziga nisbatan `wish + would` ishlatmaydi. O'z qobiliyatsizligidan afsuslanish uchun `wish + could` ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 5: `wish` va `hope` ni adashtirish
I wish you will have a great holiday. (kelajakdagi real istak)
I hope you have a great holiday.

Qoida: Kelajakda sodir bo'lishi mumkin bo'lgan real istaklar uchun `hope` ishlatiladi. `wish` esa faqat noreal yoki imkonsiz holatlar uchun.

🚫 XATO 6: Hozirgi zamon istagi uchun `was` ishlatish
I wish I was a millionaire.
I wish I were a millionaire.

Qoida: Hozirgi zamon haqidagi noreal istaklarda `to be` fe'li barcha shaxslar uchun odatda `were` shaklida ishlatiladi. Bu grammatik jihatdan to'g'riroq hisoblanadi.

🚫 XATO 7: `could` o'rniga `can` ishlatish
I wish I can fly.
I wish I could fly.

Qoida: Hozirgi zamondagi qobiliyatsizlikdan afsuslanishni bildirishda 'can' ning o'tgan zamon shakli 'could' ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: O'tgan zamon afsusida `have` ishlatish
I wish I have studied harder last year.
I wish I had studied harder last year.

Qoida: O'tgan zamon afsuslari uchun faqat Past Perfect (`had + V3`) ishlatiladi, Present Perfect (`have + V3`) emas.

🚫 XATO 9: `would have` ni noto'g'ri ishlatish
I wish you would have come to the party yesterday.
I wish you had come to the party yesterday.

Qoida: O'tgan zamon afsuslarida (boshqa birovga nisbatan bo'lsa ham) `would have` ishlatilmaydi. Faqat `had + V3` strukturasi to'g'ri keladi.

🚫 XATO 10: O'zgarmas holatlarda `would` ishlatish
I wish the weather would be sunny.
I wish the weather were sunny.

Qoida: `would` odatda o'zgartirish mumkin bo'lgan harakatlar yoki odatlarga nisbatan shikoyat uchun ishlatiladi. Ob-havo kabi biz o'zgartira olmaydigan holatlarga nisbatan `Past Simple` (bu yerda `were`) ishlatilgani ma'qul.

🚫 XATO 11: `wish` dan keyin to'g'ridan-to'g'ri infinitive
I wish to go home.
I want to go home.

Qoida: `wish` dan keyin darhol `to + infinitive` kelmaydi. Bu holatda "xohlamoq" ma'nosini `want` beradi. `wish` dan keyin doim ergash gap (clause) kelishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 12: `I wish I didn't have to...` o'rniga `I wish I wouldn't...`
I wish I wouldn't have to go to school tomorrow.
I wish I didn't have to go to school tomorrow.

Qoida: Kelajakdagi majburiyatdan norozilikni bildirish uchun `wouldn't` emas, balki `didn't have to` ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 13: Savol shaklida xato
Do you wish you would be taller?
Do you wish you were taller?

Qoida: Savol shaklida ham `wish` dan keyingi qismning zamoni o'zgarmaydi. Hozirgi zamon istagi bo'lgani uchun Past Simple (`were`) kerak.

🚫 XATO 14: Boshqa shaxs uchun `could` ishlatish
I wish my father could stop smoking.
I wish my father would stop smoking.

Qoida: Boshqa shaxsning o'zgartirishini xohlaydigan odati yoki harakati uchun `would` ishlatiladi. `could` esa o'sha shaxsning qobiliyati haqida bo'lardi.

🚫 XATO 15: `that` ni tushirib qoldirmaslik
I wish that I had more time. (Xato emas, lekin kam ishlatiladi)
I wish I had more time.

Qoida: `wish` dan keyin `that` bog'lovchisini ishlatish grammatik jihatdan xato emas, lekin zamonaviy ingliz tilida deyarli har doim tushirib qoldiriladi.
