WH- Clauses: Asosiy Qoidalar

1-qism: Vaqt (when) va Joy (where) ergash gaplari

Bu so'zlar biror otga aniqlik kirituvchi relative clause yasash uchun ishlatiladi.

  • When: Vaqtni bildiruvchi otlardan (the day, the time, the moment, the year) keyin ishlatiladi.
  • Where: Joyni bildiruvchi otlardan (the place, the house, the city, the room) keyin ishlatiladi.

I remember the day when we first met. (Biz birinchi uchrashgan kunni eslayman.)

This is the place where I grew up. (Bu men o'sgan joy.)

2-qism: Umumlashtiruvchi "-ever" so'zlari

-ever qo'shimchasi "har qanday", "kim/nima/qachon/qayerda bo'lishidan qat'iy nazar" degan ma'nolarni beradi va odatda gapda ot (noun clause) vazifasini bajaradi.

  • Whoever: "har kimki", "kim bo'lsa ham". Shaxslarga nisbatan ishlatiladi.
  • Whatever: "har nimaki", "nima bo'lsa ham". Narsalarga nisbatan ishlatiladi.
  • Whichever: "qaysi biri bo'lsa ham". Cheklangan tanlov orasidan biriga ishora qiladi.
  • Whenever: "har qachonki", "qachon bo'lsa ham". Vaqtga ishora qiladi.
  • Wherever: "har qayerdaki", "qayerda bo'lsa ham". Joyga ishora qiladi.
  • However: "qanchalik ... bo'lmasin". Sifat yoki ravishning darajasini bildiradi.

However ishlatilishi

However boshqa "-ever" so'zlaridan farqli o'laroq, o'zi yolg'iz kelmaydi. Undan keyin sifat (adjective) yoki ravish (adverb) keladi.

However hard you study, there is always more to learn. (Qanchalik ko'p o'qisang ham, o'rganish uchun doim ko'p narsa bor.)

You can't finish it, however fast you are. (Qanchalik tez bo'lsang ham, sen buni tugata olmaysan.)

Whatever vs. Whichever

Ikkalasi ham narsalarga ishora qilsa-da, ular orasida tanlov doirasiga ko'ra farq bor.

So'zMa'nosi va ishlatilishiMisol
Whatever Cheksiz tanlov. "Har nimaiki" degan ma'noni beradi. You can eat whatever you want from the menu. (Menyudan xohlagan narsangni yeyishing mumkin.)
Whichever Cheklangan tanlov. "Qaysi biri bo'lsa ham" degan ma'noni beradi (odatda 2-3 ta narsa orasidan). There are tea and coffee. You can drink whichever you prefer. (Choy va kofe bor. Qaysi birini afzal ko'rsang, ichaver.)

WH- Clauses Gapdagi Vazifalari

Ot Ergash Gap (Ega vazifasida)

Whoever/Whatever ... + Fe'l ...

Whoever comes late will miss the show.

Kim kech kelsa, shouni o'tkazib yuboradi.

Whatever she said was true.

U nima degan bo'lsa, hammasi rost edi.

Ot Ergash Gap (To'ldiruvchi vazifasida)

Ega + Fe'l + whatever/whoever ...

You can do whatever you want.

Siz xohlagan narsangizni qilishingiz mumkin.

The company will hire whoever is best for the job.

Kompaniya ish uchun kim eng yaxshi bo'lsa, o'shani ishga oladi.

Ravish Ergash Gap (Adverbial Clause)

Bosh gap + whenever/wherever ...

He feels happy whenever he sees her.

U qachonki uni ko'rsa, xursand bo'ladi.

I will find you wherever you go.

Qayerga borsang ham, men seni topaman.

However + Sifat/Ravish

However + adj/adv + S + V, ...

However hard he tried, he couldn't open the door.

U qanchalik qattiq harakat qilmasin, eshikni ocha olmadi.

However carefully you drive, accidents can happen.

Mashinani qanchalik ehtiyotkor haydasangiz ham, baxtsiz hodisalar yuz berishi mumkin.

WH- Clauses Misollari

Whoever (kim bo'lsa ham)

Whoever enters the room first may have lunch with us.

Xonaga kim birinchi bo'lib kirsa, o'sha biz bilan tushlik qilishi mumkin.

The boss will hire whoever impresses him in the interview.

Boss intervyuda kim uni hayratga solsa, o'shani ishga oladi.

Whatever (nima bo'lsa ham)

The people were saying whatever was on their mind.

Odamlar xayoliga nima kelsa, o'shani aytishayotgan edi.

You can choose whatever movie you made, I'm fine with it.

Siz qilgan nimaiki film bo'lsa, tanlang, menga roziman.

Whichever (qaysi biri bo'lsa ham)

You can choose whichever color you like.

Sizga qaysi rang yoqsa, o'shani tanlashingiz mumkin.

Take whichever book you want from the shelf.

Javondan qaysi kitobni xohlasangiz, o'shani oling.

Whenever (qachon bo'lsa ham)

You can come over whenever you want.

Qachon xohlasangiz, kelishingiz mumkin.

Feel free to visit me whenever you're in town.

Qachon shaharda bo'lsangiz, bemalol menikiga tashrif buyuring.

Wherever (qayerda bo'lsa ham)

Put your keys wherever you can easily find them.

Kalitlaringizni qayerda osongina topsa bo'ladigan bo'lsa, o'sha joyga qo'ying.

The guests can sit wherever they feel comfortable.

Mehmonlar o'zlarini qayerda qulay his qilishsa, o'sha joyda o'tirishlari mumkin.

However (qanchalik ... bo'lmasin)

However hard you try, mistakes can happen.

Qanchalik urinmang, xatolar bo'lishi mumkin.

However many times you fall, get back up and try again.

Qanchalik ko'p marta yiqilib tushsang ham, o'rningdan turib yana harakat qil.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (WH- Clauses)

🚫 XATO 1: `Whoever` vs. `Whomever` adashtirish
Give the prize to whoever you think deserves it.
Give the prize to whomever you think deserves it.

Qoida: Agar olmosh ergash gapning to'ldiruvchisi bo'lsa ('you think *he* deserves it' emas, 'you think *him*...'), 'whomever' ishlatiladi. Ammo zamonaviy ingliz tilida 'whoever' ko'proq ishlatiladi. Agar ergash gapning egasi bo'lsa (Whoever deserves it...), har doim 'whoever' ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 2: `Whatever` vs. `Whichever`
There are two options. Choose whatever you like.
There are two options. Choose whichever you like.

Qoida: Tanlov cheklangan bo'lsa (ikki, uchta narsa orasida), har doim 'whichever' ishlatiladi. 'Whatever' esa cheklanmagan tanlov uchun.

🚫 XATO 3: `where` vs. `wherever`
This is the hotel wherever we stayed.
This is the hotel where we stayed.

Qoida: 'where' aniq bir joyga ishora qiladi. 'wherever' esa 'har qanday joy' degani va noaniq joyga ishora qiladi (Sit wherever you want).

🚫 XATO 4: `However`ni noto'g'ri ishlatish
However you are smart, you can make mistakes.
However smart you are, you can make mistakes.

Qoida: 'However'dan keyin darrov sifat (smart) yoki ravish kelishi kerak, keyin ega va kesim.

🚫 XATO 5: Ot ergash gapdan keyin vergul
Whatever you decide, is fine with me.
Whatever you decide is fine with me.

Qoida: Agar WH-clause gapning egasi vazifasida kelsa, undan keyin vergul qo'yilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 6: Shaxs uchun 'whatever' ishlatish
You can ask whatever is at the front desk.
You can ask whoever is at the front desk.

Qoida: Shaxslarga nisbatan 'har kimki' ma'nosida 'whoever' ishlatiladi, 'whatever' emas.

🚫 XATO 7: `when` vs. `whenever`
I will be happy when you visit. (har qanday vaqtda)
I will be happy whenever you visit.

Qoida: 'when' aniq bir vaqtga ishora qiladi. 'whenever' esa 'har qachonki' degan ma'noni beradi.

🚫 XATO 8: So'roq belgisi bilan adashtirish
I know what is your name?
I know what your name is.

Qoida: WH-clause boshqa gapning ichida kelsa, u so'roq emas, darak gap tartibida bo'ladi (ega + kesim).

🚫 XATO 9: `-ever` bilan `that` ishlatish
He will do whatever that you ask.
He will do whatever you ask.

Qoida: `-ever` bilan tugaydigan so'zlardan keyin `that` ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 10: `however` vs. `but`
I am tired, however I will help you. (Ma'no xato emas, lekin uslubda)
I am tired, but I will help you.
However tired I am, I will help you.

Qoida: Garchi 'however' "ammo" ma'nosida kelsa ham, u ko'pincha gap boshida yoki ikki gap o'rtasida nuqtali vergul bilan keladi. 'but' bog'lovchisi esa oddiyroq va keng tarqalgan. 'however'ning "qanchalik...bo'lmasin" ma'nosini unutmaslik kerak.
