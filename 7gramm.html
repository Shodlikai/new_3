Modal Fe'llar: Majburiyat va Maslahat - Mukammal Qo'llanma

Must, Have to, Should, Ought to, Had better va ularning barcha nozik jihatlari

0%

Asosiy Qoidalar

Must / Mustn't

Must kuchli majburiyat (ko'pincha shaxsiy, ichki his-tuyg'udan kelib chiquvchi) yoki qat'iy qoidani bildiradi. Mustn't esa qat'iy taqiqni ifodalaydi.

  • Majburiyat: I must finish this report by Friday. (Men bu hisobotni juma kungacha tugatishim shart.)
  • Qoida/Qonun: You must wear a seatbelt. (Siz xavfsizlik kamarini taqishingiz shart.)
  • Taqiq: You mustn't smoke here. (Bu yerda chekish qat'iyan man etiladi.)

Have to / Don't have to

Have to tashqi majburiyatni (qoida, vaziyat, boshqa odamning talabi) bildiradi. Don't/Doesn't have to esa majburiyat yo'qligini, ya'ni biror ishni qilish shart emasligini bildiradi.

  • Tashqi majburiyat: I have to work on Saturdays. (Men shanba kunlari ishlashim kerak. - Ishxonamning qoidasi)
  • O'tgan zamon: We had to leave early yesterday. (Biz kecha erta ketishimizga to'g'ri keldi.)
  • Majburiyat yo'qligi: You don't have to come if you're busy. (Agar band bo'lsangiz, kelishingiz shart emas.)

Should / Ought to

Bu ikki fe'l deyarli bir xil ma'noda bo'lib, maslahat, tavsiya yoki biror narsa to'g'ri yoki noto'g'ri ekanligini aytish uchun ishlatiladi. Ought to biroz rasmiyroq va axloqiy majburiyatlarga ko'proq ishora qiladi.

  • Maslahat: You should see a doctor. (Shifokorga ko'rinishingiz kerak.)
  • Tanqid (o'tgan zamon): You should have told me the truth. (Menga haqiqatni aytishing kerak edi.)
  • Axloqiy majburiyat: We ought to respect our elders. (Biz kattalarni hurmat qilishimiz kerak.)

Had better

Had better ('d better) kuchli maslahat yoki ogohlantirishni bildiradi. Agar bu maslahatga amal qilinmasa, yomon oqibat kelib chiqishi mumkinligi nazarda tutiladi. U faqat hozirgi va kelasi zamonga oid vaziyatlar uchun ishlatiladi.

  • Ogohlantirish: You'd better hurry, or you'll miss the train. (Yaxshisi shoshil, aks holda poyezddan qolib ketasan.)
  • Kuchli maslahat: It's cold outside. You had better wear a coat. (Tashqari sovuq. Palto kiyganingiz ma'qul.)

Nozik Farqlar

Mustn't vs Don't have to (Eng muhim farq!)

Bu modal fe'llar orasidagi eng ko'p adashtiriladigan va ma'noni butunlay o'zgartirib yuboradigan farqdir.

Fe'lMa'nosiNatijasiMisol
Mustn'tTaqiqlangan (It's forbidden). Bu ishni qilish mumkin emas. Qilsangiz, qoida buziladi.Qilmaslik kerak.You mustn't talk in the library. (Kutubxonada gaplashish mumkin emas.)
Don't have toMajburiy emas (It's not necessary). Bu ishni qilish shart emas. Agar xohlasangiz, qilishingiz mumkin.Qilsangiz ham, qilmasangiz ham bo'ladi.You don't have to whisper. This is a cafe. (Pichirlashingiz shart emas. Bu kafe.)

Must vs Have to

Ikkala fe'l ham majburiyatni bildiradi, lekin ularning kelib chiqishi (motivatsiyasi) farqlanadi.

Fe'lKelib chiqishiMisol
MustIchki majburiyat. Gapiruvchining o'z his-tuyg'ulari, fikri yoki shaxsiy qoidasidan kelib chiqadi.I must stop eating so much sugar. (O'zim uchun qaror qildim.)
Have toTashqi majburiyat. Tashqi kuchlar, qoidalar, qonunlar, boshqa odamlarning talabi.I have to stop eating sugar because the doctor told me to. (Shifokor aytdi.)

Should vs Had better

Ikkalasi ham maslahat beradi, lekin kuchi va konteksti har xil.

Fe'lMa'nosiKontekstMisol
ShouldUmumiy maslahat. "Shunday qilsang yaxshi bo'lardi" degan ma'noda.Har qanday vaziyatda.You should visit Paris one day. (Bir kun Parijga borishing kerak.)
Had betterKuchli maslahat/Ogohlantirish. "Shunday qilmasang, yomon bo'ladi" degan ma'noda.Aniq bir vaziyatda va yomon oqibat ehtimoli bo'lganda.The weather is getting worse. We'd better go home now. (Havo yomonlashyapti. Yaxshisi hozir uyga ketaylik.)

Modal Fe'llar Strukturalari

Must (Majburiyat)

Ega + must + V (asosiy shakli)

1. I must finish this work today.

Men bu ishni bugun tugatishim shart.

2. You must follow the rules.

Siz qoidalarga rioya qilishingiz shart.

Mustn't (Taqiq)

Ega + mustn't + V (asosiy shakli)

1. You mustn't park here.

Bu yerga mashina qo'yishingiz mumkin emas.

2. We mustn't be late.

Biz kechikmasligimiz kerak.

Have to / Has to (Majburiyat)

Ega + have to / has to + V (asosiy shakli)

1. I have to go now.

Men hozir ketishim kerak.

2. She has to work on weekends.

U dam olish kunlari ishlashi kerak.

Had to (O'tgan zamon majburiyati)

Ega + had to + V (asosiy shakli)

1. We had to cancel the trip.

Biz sayohatni bekor qilishimiz kerak edi.

2. He had to apologize.

U uzr so'rashi kerak edi.

Don't / Doesn't have to (Majburiyat yo'qligi)

Ega + don't / doesn't have to + V (asosiy shakli)

1. You don't have to pay.

Siz pul to'lashingiz shart emas.

2. She doesn't have to come.

U kelishi shart emas.

Should / Ought to (Maslahat)

Ega + should / ought to + V (asosiy shakli)

1. You should listen to your parents.

Siz ota-onangizni tinglashingiz kerak.

2. He ought to apologize.

U uzr so'rashi kerak.

Had better (Kuchli maslahat)

Ega + had better + V (asosiy shakli)

1. You had better study.

Yaxshisi, o'qishingiz kerak.

2. We'd better leave now.

Yaxshisi, hozir ketganimiz ma'qul.

Should have + V3 (O'tgan zamon)

Ega + should + have + V3

1. I should have studied harder.

Men qattiqroq o'qishim kerak edi (lekin o'qimadim).

2. You shouldn't have told her.

Siz unga aytmasligingiz kerak edi (lekin aytdingiz).

Misollar To'plami

Kuchli Majburiyat va Taqiq

I must remember to buy a gift for her birthday.

Uning tug'ilgan kuniga sovg'a olishni eslab qolishim shart.

Pilots have to get a special license to fly a plane.

Uchuvchilar samolyotni boshqarish uchun maxsus litsenziya olishlari kerak.

You mustn't touch that wire. It's dangerous.

U simga teginishing mumkin emas. U xavfli.

Yesterday, I had to work late to finish the project.

Kecha loyihani tugatish uchun kechgacha ishlashimga to'g'ri keldi.

This is a secret. You mustn't tell anybody.

Bu sir. Hech kimga aytmasliging kerak.

Maslahat va Tavsiya

You should drink more water every day.

Har kuni ko'proq suv ichishing kerak.

He ought to be more careful when driving.

U mashina haydayotganda ehtiyotkorroq bo'lishi kerak.

I think we should check the information again.

O'ylaymanki, biz ma'lumotni yana bir bor tekshirishimiz kerak.

You shouldn't believe everything you read online.

Internetda o'qigan hamma narsaga ishonmasliging kerak.

She ought to have listened to my advice.

U mening maslahatimga quloq solishi kerak edi.

Majburiyat Yo'qligi

It's Sunday, so I don't have to go to work.

Bugun Yakshanba, shuning uchun ishga borishim shart emas.

She doesn't have to cook tonight. We can order pizza.

U bugun ovqat qilishi shart emas. Pizza buyurtma qilishimiz mumkin.

The museum entrance was free, so we didn't have to buy tickets.

Muzeyga kirish tekin edi, shuning uchun chipta sotib olishimizga to'g'ri kelmadi.

You don't have to finish the book if you don't like it.

Agar yoqmasa, kitobni oxirigacha o'qishing shart emas.

He is rich. He doesn't have to worry about money.

U boy. Pul haqida qayg'urishi shart emas.

Ogohlantirish va O'tgan Zamondagi Afsus

You'd better leave now or you will be late.

Yaxshisi hozir jo'na, aks holda kech qolasan.

I should have studied harder for the test. I failed.

Testga qattiqroq o'qishim kerak edi. Men yiqildim.

He had better not forget to call her.

Yaxshisi u unga qo'ng'iroq qilishni unutmasin.

You shouldn't have spent so much money.

Buncha ko'p pul sarflamasliging kerak edi.

We had better check the weather before we go camping.

Lagerga borishdan oldin ob-havoni tekshirganimiz ma'qul.

Muhim Qoidalar va Keng Tarqalgan Xatolar

🚫 XATO 1: `Mustn't` vs `Don't have to` adashtirish
Vaziyat: Siz muzeydasiz. Ba'zi xonalarga kirish tekin, ba'zilariga esa taqiqlangan.
To enter this room, you mustn't pay. (Bu xonaga kirish uchun pul to'lamasligingiz shart.) - Ma'no xato!
To enter this room, you don't have to pay. (Bu xonaga kirish uchun pul to'lashingiz shart emas.)
🔍 Sababi:

`Mustn't` - bu TAQIQ. (Kirish taqiqlangan, suratga olish mumkin emas).
`Don't have to` - bu MAJBURIYAT YO'QLIGI. (To'lash shart emas, lekin xohlasangiz to'lang; forma kiyish shart emas, lekin kiyishingiz mumkin).

❌ XATO 2: O'tgan zamonda `Must` ishlatish
Yesterday, I musted go to the doctor.
Yesterday, I had to go to the doctor.
🔍 Sababi:

Must modal fe'lining o'tgan zamon shakli yo'q. O'tgan zamondagi majburiyatni ifodalash uchun har doim had to ishlatiladi.

🤔 XATO 3: `Had better` va `Should` ni adashtirish
Umumiy maslahat bermoqchisiz: "Odamlar ko'proq sport bilan shug'ullanishi kerak".
People had better exercise more. (Noto'g'ri kontekst)
People should exercise more.
🔍 Sababi:

`Should` - umumiy, har doimga oid maslahatlar uchun.
`Had better` - aniq bir vaziyatda, yomon oqibatni oldini olish uchun beriladigan kuchli maslahat/ogohlantirish uchun (masalan, "Yomg'ir yog'yapti, yaxshisi soyabon ol" - `You'd better take an umbrella`).

⚙️ XATO 4: Struktura xatolari
He doesn't has to come.
You should to call her.
He doesn't have to come.
You should call her.
🔍 Sababi:

do/does/did yordamchi fe'llaridan keyin har doim fe'lning asosiy shakli (have) keladi.
Barcha modal fe'llardan keyin (ought to dan tashqari) to yuklamasi ishlatilmaydi.
