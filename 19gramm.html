O'zlashtirma Gap (Indirect Speech) Qoidalari

Umumiy tushuncha

Bir odamning gapining mazmunini yetkazish o'zlashtirma gap (Indirect Speech) deyiladi. O'zlashtirma gap oldida odatda that bog'lovchisi qo'yiladi, ammo bu bog'lovchi ko'pincha tushib qoladi.

Direct: He said, "I am busy." (U dedi: "Men bandman")

Indirect: He said (that) he was busy. (U band ekanligini aytdi)

Zamonlarning surilishi (Backshift)

Agar bosh gapdagi fe'l o'tgan zamonda bo'lsa (He said, told, asked), ko'chirma gapdagi zamonlar bir pog'ona orqaga (o'tmishga) suriladi.

  • Present Simple → Past Simple
  • Present Continuous → Past Continuous
  • Past Simple / Present Perfect → Past Perfect
  • Will → Would

Olmoshlarning o'zgarishi

Gapiruvchi shaxsga qarab kishilik olmoshlari o'zgaradi.

Tom said, "I like my car."

Tom said (that) he liked his car.

O'zlashtirma Gap Strukturalari

Darak Gap (Statements)

S + said (that) + S + Verb (shifted).

He said, "I work hard."

→ He said (that) he worked hard.

She said, "I have finished."

→ She said she had finished.

Maxsus So'roq Gap (Wh- Questions)

S + asked + Wh-word + S + Verb (shifted).

"Where do you live?" he asked.

→ He asked me where I lived.

"When will they come?" she asked.

→ She asked when they would come.

*Eslatma: So'roq belgisi va yordamchi fe'llar (do/did) tushib qoladi.

Umumiy So'roq Gap (Yes/No Questions)

S + asked + if / whether + S + Verb.

"Do you like coffee?" he asked.

→ He asked if I liked coffee.

"Is she sleeping?" mum asked.

→ Mum asked whether she was sleeping.

Buyruq va Iltimos (Commands)

S + told/asked + Obj + to + V1.

"Open the door," he said.

→ He told me to open the door.

"Don't talk," teacher said.

→ The teacher told us not to talk.

Darslikdan Olingan Misollar

Darak Gaplar (Statements)

He said that the ship would arrive at the end of the week.

U kemani haftaning oxirida kelishini aytdi.

The teacher said that the goods were delivered yesterday.

O'qituvchi tovarlar kecha yetkazib berilganligini aytdi.

She said that she had met John when she was crossing the road.

U yo'lni kesib o'tayotganida Jonni uchratganligini aytdi.

So'roq Gaplar (Wh- Questions)

He asked me where they lived.

U mendan ularning qayerda yashashini so'radi.

He asked me why I had come so late.

U mendan nima uchun bunchalik kech kelganimni so'radi.

He asked me who had shown me his work.

U mendan ishini menga kim ko'rsatganligini so'radi.

Yordamchi Fe'lli So'roqlar (Yes/No Questions)

He asked me if I lived in the country.

U mendan mamlakatda yashash-yashamasligimizni so'radi.

He asked me whether I would be there the next day.

U mendan keyingi kuni o'sha yerda bo'lish-bo'lmasligimni so'radi.

The doctor wanted to know if I had had the symptoms for a long time.

Doktor uzoq vaqtdan beri menda belgilar bo'lgan-bo'lmaganini bilishni xohladi.

Vaqt o'zgarishiga doir

He said that he would learn English the following day.

U kelasi kuni ingliz tilini o'rganishini aytdi.

Helen told me that she was writing a letter the day before.

Helen kecha xat yozayotganligini menga aytgandi.

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Indirect Speech)

🚫 XATO 1: So'roq gap tartibini saqlab qolish
He asked me where did I go.
He asked me where I went.

Qoida: O'zlashtirma gapda so'roq gap tuzilishi (yordamchi fe'l egadan oldin) ishlatilmaydi. Gap darak gap tartibiga (Ega + Kesim) o'tadi.

🚫 XATO 2: Zamonni orqaga surmaslik
He said he is busy.
He said he was busy.

Qoida: Agar bosh gap o'tgan zamonda bo'lsa (said/told), ergash gapdagi zamon ham o'tgan zamonga o'tishi shart.

🚫 XATO 3: Vaqt so'zlarini o'zgartirmaslik
He said he would come tomorrow.
He said he would come the next day.

Qoida: O'tgan zamondagi voqea haqida gapirilganda, "tomorrow" (ertaga) so'zi mantiqan noto'g'ri bo'ladi. Uning o'rniga "the next day" (keyingi kun) ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 4: "Said" va "Told" ni adashtirish
He said me that... / He told that...
He said (to me) that... / He told me that...

Qoida: Tell fe'lidan keyin doim shaxs (me, him, her) kelishi shart. Say fe'lidan keyin esa shaxs kelmasligi mumkin, agar kelsa "to" bilan ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 5: If/Whether tushirib qoldirish
He asked me did I like it.
He asked me if I liked it.

Qoida: Umumiy so'roq gaplarni (Yes/No questions) o'zlashtirmaga aylantirganda if yoki whether bog'lovchisi shart.
