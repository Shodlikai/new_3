Majhul Nisbat Qoidalari

Passive Voice nima va qachon ishlatiladi?

Passive Voice (Majhul nisbat)da gapning fokusi ish-harakatni bajaruvchiga (agent) emas, balki o'sha harakat qaratilgan obyektga tushadi. Ya'ni, ishni kim qilgani emas, nima ish qilingani muhimroq bo'ladi.

Asosiy qo'llanilish holatlari:
  • Harakatni bajaruvchi noma'lum bo'lganda: My car was stolen. (Mashinam o'g'irlandi. Kim o'g'irlagani noma'lum).
  • Harakatni bajaruvchi ahamiyatsiz bo'lganda: This bridge was built in 1998. (Bu ko'prik 1998-yilda qurilgan. Kim qurgani muhim emas).
  • Harakatni bajaruvchi aniq bo'lganda: The suspect was arrested. (Gumonlanuvchi hibsga olindi. Tabiiyki, politsiya tomonidan).

Agar harakat bajaruvchisini aytish shart bo'lsa, gap oxirida by predlogi bilan qo'shiladi: The novel was written by the author.

Passive Voice Zamonlar Bo'yicha Yasalishi va Ishlatilishi

Present Simple Passive (is/are + V3)

Ishlatilishi: Umumiy haqiqatlar, takrorlanib turuvchi odatiy ishlar, qonun-qoidalar va jarayonlarni tasvirlash uchun. Harakatning har doim yoki odatda shunday bajarilishini ko'rsatadi.

Misol: English is spoken all over the world. (Ingliz tilida butun dunyoda gaplashiladi - bu umumiy haqiqat).

Past Simple Passive (was/were + V3)

Ishlatilishi: O'tgan zamonda aniq bir vaqtda sodir bo'lgan va tugallangan ish-harakatlar uchun. Gapda ko'pincha yesterday, last week, in 2010 kabi o'tgan zamonga ishora qiluvchi so'zlar bo'ladi.

Misol: A young child was stolen yesterday. (Kecha yosh bolani o'g'irlab ketishdi - harakat kecha sodir bo'ldi va tugadi).

Future Simple Passive (will be + V3)

Ishlatilishi: Kelajakda sodir bo'ladigan ish-harakatlar uchun. Gapda ko'pincha tomorrow, next week, soon kabi kelasi zamonga ishora qiluvchi so'zlar bo'ladi.

Misol: The new software will be installed tomorrow. (Yangi dasturiy ta'minot ertaga o'rnatiladi).

Present Continuous Passive (is/are being + V3)

Ishlatilishi: Ayni nutq so'zlanayotgan paytda yoki hozirgi vaqt atrofida davom etayotgan ish-harakatlar uchun. "Hozir qilinayapti" degan ma'noni beradi.

Misol: Please be quiet. A new movie is being filmed on the set. (Iltimos, jim bo'ling. Suratga olish maydonchasida yangi film suratga olinayapti - ayni hozir).

Past Continuous Passive (was/were being + V3)

Ishlatilishi: O'tgan zamonda aniq bir paytda davom etayotgan bo'lgan ish-harakatlar uchun. Ko'pincha when yoki while bilan boshqa bir ish-harakat foni sifatida keladi.

Misol: The cake was being baked when the guests arrived. (Mehmonlar yetib kelganda tort pishirilayotgan edi - pishirish jarayoni davom etayotgan edi).

Active vs. Passive va Passive Zamonlar Farqi

Active Voice (Aniq nisbat) vs. Passive Voice (Majhul nisbat)

Gapdagi urg'u (fokus) kimga yoki nimaga qaratilganiga qarab bu ikki nisbat bir-biridan farq qiladi.

XususiyatActive Voice (Aniq nisbat)Passive Voice (Majhul nisbat)
Fokus / Urg'u Ish-harakatni bajaruvchiga (Agent) qaratiladi. Ish-harakat qaratilgan obyektga qaratiladi.
Formula Ega + Fe'l + To'ldiruvchi Obyekt + "to be" + V3 (+ by Agent)
Misol Sarah bakes the cake.
(Sara tort pishiradi.)		 The cake is baked by Sarah.
(Tort Sara tomonidan pishiriladi.)

Passive Zamonlar O'rtasidagi Farqlar (Nimalarga e'tibor berish kerak?)

To'g'ri zamonni tanlash uchun gapdagi vaqtga va harakatning davomiy yoki tugallanganligiga e'tibor berish kerak. Quyidagi jadval ularni farqlashga yordam beradi.

Zamon (Tense) Formula Ishlatilish Holati (Qachon ishlatiladi?) Signal So'zlar (Kalit so'zlar)
Present Simple Passive is/are + V3 Umumiy haqiqatlar, takrorlanuvchi odatlar, qonunlar. every day, always, usually, often, sometimes
Past Simple Passive was/were + V3 O'tgan zamonda aniq vaqtda sodir bo'lib, tugagan harakat. yesterday, last week, ago, in 1999
Present Continuous Passive is/are + being + V3 Ayni paytda yoki hozirgi vaqt atrofida davom etayotgan harakat. now, at the moment, right now, Look!, Listen!
Past Continuous Passive was/were + being + V3 O'tgan zamonda aniq bir paytda davom etayotgan bo'lgan harakat. while, when..., at 5 PM yesterday

Gap tuzilmalari (Darak, Inkor, So'roq)

Present Simple Passive

(+) S + is/are + V3
(-) S + is/are not + V3
(?) Is/Are + S + V3?

(+) English is spoken here.

(-) A soft drink is not sold at this shop.

(?) Is this game watched in the city?

Past Simple Passive

(+) S + was/were + V3
(-) S + was/were not + V3
(?) Was/Were + S + V3?

(+) A young child was stolen yesterday.

(-) The peach trees were not planted last week.

(?) Was a bottle of wine opened by Sam?

Future Simple Passive

(+) S + will be + V3
(-) S + will not be + V3
(?) Will + S + be + V3?

(+) My shirt will be ironed by my wife.

(-) The door will not be painted in blue.

(?) Will the windows be closed?

Present Continuous Passive

(+) S + is/are being + V3
(-) S + is/are not being + V3
(?) Is/Are + S + being + V3?

(+) This man is being awarded.

(-) The ceiling is not being painted.

(?) Are many tickets being bought?

Past Continuous Passive

(+) S + was/were being + V3
(-) S + was/were not being + V3
(?) Was/Were + S + being + V3?

(+) The cake was being baked by Mary.

(-) The song was not being sung by the singer.

(?) Was the lesson being taught by the instructor?

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar (Passive Voice)

🚫 XATO 1: `be` fe'lini unutish
The house built last year.
The house was built last year.

Qoida: Majhul nisbat har doim `be` fe'lining zamonga mos shakli bilan birga keladi. Uni tushirib qoldirish mumkin emas.

🚫 XATO 2: Fe'lning noto'g'ri shakli (V1/V2)
The letter was wrote by him.
The letter was written by him.

Qoida: Majhul nisbatda asosiy fe'l har doim 3-shaklda (Past Participle - V3) bo'lishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 3: `being` ni unutish (Continuous)
The car is repaired at the moment.
The car is being repaired at the moment.

Qoida: Davomiy zamonlarning (Present/Past Continuous) majhul nisbatida `be` va `V3` o'rtasida `being` bo'lishi shart.

🚫 XATO 4: O'timsiz fe'llarni majhulda ishlatish
A serious accident was happened.
A serious accident happened.

Qoida: `happen`, `arrive`, `die`, `sleep` kabi o'timsiz fe'llar (o'zidan keyin to'ldiruvchi talab qilmaydigan) majhul nisbatda ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 5: Ega va `be` fe'li moslashmasligi
The books was published last year.
The books were published last year.

Qoida: `be` fe'li (is/are, was/were) gapning egasiga (bu yerda 'books' - ko'plik) mos bo'lishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 6: Future Passive'da `be` ni unutish
The problem will solved tomorrow.
The problem will be solved tomorrow.

Qoida: Kelasi zamon majhul nisbatida `will` va `V3` o'rtasida `be` bo'lishi shart.

🚫 XATO 7: `by` va `with` ni adashtirish
The door was opened by a key.
The door was opened with a key.

Qoida: Harakatni bajaruvchi shaxs yoki narsa uchun `by` ishlatiladi. Ishni bajarishda ishlatilgan asbob-uskuna uchun `with` ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 8: So'roq shaklida noto'g'ri so'z tartibi
The car was repaired yesterday?
Was the car repaired yesterday?

Qoida: So'roq gaplarda `be` fe'li (was, is, will...) egadan oldinga o'tadi.

🚫 XATO 9: Perfect zamonlarda `been` ni unutish
The work has finished. (passive ma'nosida)
The work has been finished.

Qoida: Present/Past Perfect zamonlarining majhul nisbatida `have/has/had` va `V3` o'rtasida `been` bo'lishi shart.

🚫 XATO 10: `Active` va `Passive`ni aralashtirish
The company was built the bridge.
The company built the bridge. / The bridge was built by the company.

Qoida: Agar gapda harakat bajaruvchisi ega bo'lib kelsa (The company), fe'l Aniq nisbatda bo'lishi kerak. Agar harakat obyekti ega bo'lib kelsa (The bridge), fe'l Majhul nisbatda bo'lishi kerak.
