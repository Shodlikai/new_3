Relative Clause Qoidalari

Relative Pronouns (Nisbiy olmoshlar)

Relative clauses (ergash gaplar) bosh gapdagi biror otni (shaxs yoki narsani) aniqlash yoki u haqida qo'shimcha ma'lumot berish uchun ishlatiladi. Ular quyidagi nisbiy olmoshlar bilan boshlanadi:

  • Who: Odamlar uchun (ega vazifasida).
  • Whom: Odamlar uchun (to'ldiruvchi vazifasida, rasmiy uslubda).
  • Which: Narsalar va hayvonlar uchun.
  • That: Odamlar, narsalar va hayvonlar uchun (asosan defining clauses'da).
  • Whose: Egalikni bildirish uchun (odam va narsalarga nisbatan).

Whose - Egalik nisbiy olmoshi

Whose biror kishi yoki narsaning boshqa bir narsaga egaligini ko'rsatadi. "-ning" qo'shimchasiga to'g'ri keladi.

The man whose car was stolen went to the police. (Mashinasi o'g'irlangan kishi politsiyaga bordi.)

I know a student whose hair is yellow. (Men sochi sariq bo'lgan talabani taniyman.)

Predloglar bilan ishlatilishi (Clauses with Prepositions)

Agar ergash gapda predlog bo'lsa, u odatda gap oxirida keladi (norasmiy uslubda) yoki nisbiy olmoshdan oldin keladi (rasmiy uslubda).

  • Norasmiy: This is the house which I lived in.
  • Rasmiy: This is the house in which I lived.

Eslatma: Predlog olmoshdan oldin kelganda that ishlatilmaydi.

Qisqartirilgan ergash gaplar (Reduced Relative Clauses)

Agar ergash gapdagi fe'l davomiy (continuous) yoki majhul (passive) nisbatda bo'lsa, who/which/that olmoshini va be yordamchi fe'lini tushirib qoldirish mumkin.

  • Active (-ing): The baby who is crying in the room is hungry. → The baby crying in the room is hungry.
  • Passive (-ed/V3): The book which was written by him is famous. → The book written by him is famous.

Superlative/Ordinal/Only/Last + to-infinitive

Sifatning orttirma darajasi (superlatives), tartib sonlar (ordinals) yoki the only/the last so'zlaridan keyin kelgan otni aniqlash uchun who/that o'rniga to-infinitive ishlatilishi mumkin.

Mike was the first man who left the ship. → Mike was the first man to leave the ship. (Kemani tark etgan birinchi odam Mayk edi.)

Sarah is the cleverest student that studies at this school. → Sarah is the cleverest student to study at this school. (Sara bu maktabda o'qiydigan eng aqlli o'quvchi.)

To'liq va Qisqartirilgan Gaplar (Full vs Reduced Clauses)

Qisqartirish faqat ma'lum sharoitlarda mumkin.

To'liq GapQisqartirilgan GapShart
The man who is sitting there is my father. The man sitting there is my father. Active voice (faol nisbat)

Relative Clause Strukturalari

Who/Which/That (Ega o'rnida)

Noun + who/which/that + Verb ...

The student who studies at Harvard reads one book a day.

Garvardda o'qiydigan talaba kuniga bitta kitob o'qiydi.

Whom/Which/That (To'ldiruvchi o'rnida)

Noun + (whom/...) + Subject + Verb

The man (whom) I saw was tall.

Men ko'rgan kishi novcha edi.

Whose (Egalik)

Noun + whose + Noun + Verb

The student whose hair is yellow.

Sochi sariq bo'lgan talaba.

The singer whose voice is beautiful.

Ovozi chiroyli bo'lgan qo'shiqchi.

Predlog bilan

Noun + prep. + whom/which ...

The table on which you left your things.

Siz narsalaringizni qoldirgan stol.

Reduced Clause (-ing)

Noun + V-ing ...

The baby crying in the room is hungry.

Xonada yig'layotgan chaqaloq och.

Infinitive Clause

The first/last/... Noun + to V ...

The first man to leave the ship was Mike.

Kemani tark etgan birinchi inson Mayk edi.

Relative Clause Misollari

Whose bilan misollar

The man whose baby is crying.

Chaqalog'i yig'layotgan kishi.

The student whose hair is yellow.

Sochi sariq bo'lgan talaba.

The judge whose knowledge is good.

Bilimi yaxshi bo'lgan sudya.

Superlative/Ordinal + to Infinitive

The first man to leave the ship was Mike.

Kemani tark etgan birinchi inson Mayk edi.

The cleverest student to study at this school is Sarah.

Bu maktabda o'qiydigan eng aqlli talaba Sara.

The last woman to enter the room did not look at us.

Xonaga kirgan eng oxirgi ayol bizga qaramadi.

The fifth city to attract tourists is Milan.

Turistlarni o'ziga jalb qiladigan beshinchi shahar Milandir.

Reduced Clauses (-ing)

Who is the man speaking on the radio?

Radioda gapirayotgan kishi kim?

The textbooks from the pupils who are copying are not interesting. → The textbooks from the pupils copying are not interesting.

Ko'chirayotgan o'quvchilarning darsliklari qiziqarli emas.

Do you know the boy sitting next to me?

Mening yonimda o'tirgan bolani taniysizmi?

Gaplarni Birlashtirish

1. I saw a girl. Her hair was yellow. (Use: whose)

2. A man answered the phone. He was very polite. (Use: who)

3. The baby is crying. He is hungry. (Reduce the clause using -ing)

4. Yuri Gagarin was the first man. He flew into space. (Use: to-infinitive)

5. I bought a car. It is very fast. (Use: which)

6. She is talking to a man. Do you know him? (Use: whom)

7. We visited the museum. It was closed. (Use a non-defining clause)

8. A boy's bike was stolen. He went to the police. (Use: whose)

9. The film was made in Italy. It was a success. (Reduce using passive -ed)

10. This is the hotel. We stayed at this hotel last year. (Use: where)

Muhim Qoidalar va Xatolar

🚫 XATO 1: Olmoshlarni adashtirish (Who vs Which)
The man which works here is from Spain.
The man who works here is from Spain.

WHO: faqat odamlar uchun. WHICH: faqat narsalar va hayvonlar uchun.

🚫 XATO 2: Whose vs Who's
That's the woman who's husband is a famous actor.
That's the woman whose husband is a famous actor.

WHOSE: Egalikni bildiradi ("-ning"). WHO'S: "Who is" yoki "Who has" ning qisqartmasi.

🚫 XATO 3: Non-defining clauseda 'that' ishlatish
My car, that is very old, often breaks down.
My car, which is very old, often breaks down.

Qoida: Vergullar bilan ajratilgan (non-defining) ergash gaplarda that ishlatilmaydi. Faqat who yoki which ishlatish kerak.

🚫 XATO 4: Predlogdan keyin 'that' yoki 'who' ishlatish
The hotel in that we stayed was excellent.
The hotel in which we stayed was excellent.

Qoida: Predlogdan (in, on, at, with, to) keyin faqat which (narsalar uchun) yoki whom (odamlar uchun) keladi. that va who ishlatilmaydi.

🚫 XATO 5: Ega-kesim moslashuvi xatosi
He is one of the students who is always on time.
He is one of the students who are always on time.

Qoida: Ergash gapdagi fe'l who/which olmoshi aniqlayotgan otga moslashishi kerak. Bu yerda 'who' 'students' (ko'plik) ga ishora qilmoqda, shuning uchun fe'l ham ko'plikda (are) bo'lishi kerak.

🚫 XATO 6: Ortiqcha olmosh ishlatish
This is the book which I bought it yesterday.
This is the book which I bought yesterday.

Qoida: Nisbiy olmosh (which) allaqachon to'ldiruvchi vazifasini bajarayotgani uchun, gapda yana bir marta to'ldiruvchi olmoshni (it) ishlatish shart emas.

🚫 XATO 7: Olmoshni noto'g'ri tushirib qoldirish
The man lives next door is a doctor.
The man who lives next door is a doctor.

Qoida: Nisbiy olmoshni faqat u ergash gapning to'ldiruvchisi bo'lgandagina tushirib qoldirish mumkin. Agar u ega bo'lsa (undan keyin fe'l kelsa), uni tushirib bo'lmaydi.

🚫 XATO 8: 'What' va 'Which'ni adashtirish
I read the book what you gave me.
I read the book which/that you gave me.

Qoida: what "the thing(s) which" degan ma'noni anglatadi va o'zidan oldingi otni aniqlash uchun ishlatilmaydi. Otdan keyin which yoki that ishlatiladi.

🚫 XATO 9: Ergash gapni noto'g'ri joylashtirish
The car is in the garage that I bought last week.
The car that I bought last week is in the garage.

Qoida: Ergash gap o'zi aniqlayotgan otga iloji boricha yaqinroq turishi kerak. Birinchi misolda go'yo "garaj o'tgan hafta sotib olingandek" ma'no chiqmoqda.

🚫 XATO 10: 'The reason why' birikmasini ortiqcha ishlatish
The reason why she left the job is not clear.
The reason she left the job is not clear.

Qoida: "The reason" so'zining o'zi sababni bildiradi, shuning uchun undan keyin "why" ni ishlatish ko'p hollarda ortiqcha hisoblanadi. Faqat "The reason" yoki faqat "Why" bilan gap tuzish uslubiy jihatdan to'g'riroqdir.

💡 UMUMIY MASLAHATLAR
To'g'ri olmoshni tanlash uchun o'zingizga savol bering:
1️⃣ Shaxsmi yoki narsami? (Who vs Which)
2️⃣ Egalik bormi? ("-ning") (Whose)
3️⃣ Gap muhim ma'lumot berayaptimi yoki qo'shimchami? (Vergul kerakmi? That ishlatsa bo'ladimi?)
4️⃣ Gapni qisqartirish mumkinmi? (Active/Passive, a-la -ing/V3)
